# PS8/21 | CP9/20 Non-systemic UK banks: The Prudential Regulation Authority’s approach to new and growing banks

**PS8/21**

Published: 18 March 2026

Source: https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/publication/2020/new-and-growing-banks

---

Alongside this PS, the PRA has re-launched the [New Banks Start-up Unit webpage](https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/new-bank-start-up-unit). The updated webpage has been developed as a reference tool for firms considering setting up a bank in the UK, and for newly authorised firms, and contains information covering a range of supervisory topics. We encourage firms to use this as a key source of information when developing their business propositions and regulatory documents.
