PRA Glossary - J

joint account
means an account opened in the name of two or morepersonsover which two or morepersonshave rights that are exercised by means of the signature of one or more of thosepersons.
jointly controlled body
means a body thatfriendly societyhas joint control of in accordance with section 13 of the Friendly Societies Act 1992.
