# Solvency II: National Specific Templates LOG files

Supervisory Statement 6/18

Published on 06 July 2018

### Overview

In this Supervisory Statement (SS), the Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA) sets out its expectation of firms when completing their submission of National Specific Templates (NSTs).

This SS is relevant to all UK Solvency II firms and to the Society of Lloyd’s and its managing agents.

This SS is effective from Monday 31 December 2018.
