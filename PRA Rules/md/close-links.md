# Close Links

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to every _firm_.

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

has the meaning given in _threshold condition_ 5F(3) (Effective Supervision).

means the form referred to at [5.3.](/pra-rules/close-links#812c1f19864e4319a0ef4faff55f90a3 "5.3.")

means the form referred to at [5.2](/pra-rules/close-links#e141f9b5ebeb49e2a906280bb16d844d "5.2").

means the form referred to at [5.1](/pra-rules/close-links#962d4870ab4e4f1d82d13ef8574df019 "5.1").

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

2

###  Requirement to Notify Changes in Close Links

2.1

A _firm_ must notify (or procure that another _firm_ in its _group_ notifies) the _PRA_ that it has begun to have or ceased to have _close links_ with any _person_.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

2.2

Where a _firm_ has elected to report changes in _close links_ on a monthly basis under [3.1](/pra-rules/close-links#f9ece5add48c45fdb0aa4823b39ffd4a "3.1"), it must:

  1. (1) within fifteen _business days_ of the end of each month, report (or procure that another _firm_ in its _group_ reports) electronically for that month, by completing the _Close Links Monthly Report _and submitting it through the relevant platform provided by the _PRA_ ;
  2. (2) on a quarterly basis, submit (or procure that another _firm_ in its _group_ submits) a _group_ organisation chart, unless there have been no changes since the submission of the previous organisation chart to the _PRA_ , in which case the _group_ organisation chart is not required.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

2.3

The _Close Links Monthly Report_ must include the information specified in the form.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

2.4

Where a _firm_ has not elected to report changes in _close links_ on a monthly basis under [3.1](/pra-rules/close-links#f9ece5add48c45fdb0aa4823b39ffd4a "3.1"), it must make (or procure that another _firm_ in its _group_ makes) a notification by completing the _Close Links Notification Form_ as soon as reasonably practicable and no later than one month after it becomes aware that it has begun to have or ceased to have _close links_ with any person.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

2.5

The notification submitted under [2.4](/pra-rules/close-links#5cbd40f04a624d668f160440c3ae1c3d "2.4") must be made by completing the _Close Links Notification Form_ and must include all the relevant information specified therein.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

3

###  Election to Notify Changes in Close Links on a Monthly Basis

3.1

A _firm_ wishing to report changes in _close links_ on a monthly basis must send a written notice of election to the _firm’s_ usual supervisory contact at the _PRA_.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

3.2

A firm wishing to cease reporting changes in _close links_ on a monthly basis must send a written notice of that intention to its usual supervisory contact at the _PRA_ at least one month before changing its _close links_ reporting practice.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

4

###  Annual Close Links Report

4.1

A _firm_ must submit (or procure that another _firm_ in its _group_ submits) annually by electronic means to the _PRA_ the _Close Links Annual Report_ , within four months of the _firm’s_ _accounting reference date_.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

4.2

An unincorporated _friendly society_ is only required to submit a report under [4.1](/pra-rules/close-links#e5154d4df3074a2d88eff715efe192cb "4.1") if it is aware that it has _close links_.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*

4.3

The _Close Links Annual Report_ must include the information specified in the form.

*Effective: 03/08/2015*
