# Own Funds

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ; and
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_Additional Tier 1 instrument_

means capital instruments which meet all of the applicable conditions and requirements set out in Chapter 3 and Chapter 6 of Part Two of CRR.

_Common Equity Tier 1 instrument_

means capital instruments which meet all of the applicable conditions and requirements set out in Chapter 2 and Chapter 6 of Part Two of CRR.

legacy paid-in preference shares

means paid-in preference shares that meet the following conditions:

  1. (1) the instruments were issued prior to 18 January 2015;
  2. (2) on 31 December 2015, the instruments could be used as:
     1. (a) core tier one capital in accordance with stage A (Core tier one capital) of the capital resources table at GENPRU 2 Annex 1 of the PRA Handbook as at 31 December 2015;
     2. (b) perpetual non-cumulative preference shares in accordance with stage B (Perpetual non-cumulative preference shares) of the capital resources table at GENPRU 2 Annex 1 of the PRA Handbook as at 31 December 2015;
     3. (c) innovative tier one capital in accordance with GENPRU 2.2 of the PRA Handbook as at 31 December 2015; or
     4. (d) upper tier two capital in accordance with stage G (Upper tier two capital) of the capital resources table at GENPRU 2 Annex 1 of the PRA Handbook as at 31 December 2015;
  3. (3) the instruments are not otherwise included as Tier 1 own funds, Tier 2 own funds, or Tier 3 own funds in accordance with [Own Funds 3.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#8185b18ba3694a31b626911a1d6d4a33) to [3.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#2b3e51bb99b24013b4816c97c6fce523).

_restricted Tier 1 own funds_ __

means the items referred to in [3A.1.1(c)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1.1(e)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) and [3A.1(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0).

_Tier 2 instrument_

means capital instruments which meet all of the applicable conditions and requirements set out in Chapter 4 and Chapter 6 of Part Two of CRR.

*Effective: 02/01/2026*

2

###  Determination of Own Funds

2.1

A _firm’s_ _own funds_ comprise the sum of its _basic own funds_ and subject to [2.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#12a873b504854ba685cc8615b25767f1),  _ancillary own funds_.

**[Note: Art. 87 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

2.2

The _firm’s basic own funds_ consist of the following items:

  1. (1) the excess of assets over liabilities, less the amount of own _shares_ held by the _firm_ ; and
  2. (2) subordinated liabilities.

**[Note: Art. 88 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.3

Subject to [2.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#12a873b504854ba685cc8615b25767f1 "2.5"), the _firm’s ancillary own funds_ consist of items (other than items of _basic own funds_) which can be called up to absorb losses, including the following (to the extent that they are not items of _basic own funds_):

  1. (1) unpaid share capital or initial fund that has not been called up;
  2. (2) letters of credit and guarantees;
  3. (3) any other legally binding commitments received by the _firm_ ; and
  4. (4) for a _mutual_ , any future claims which it may have against its members by way of a call for supplementary contribution within the next 12 _months_.

**[Note: Art. 89(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.4

Where an item of _ancillary own funds_ becomes paid in or called up, the proceeds paid in or the amount due in respect of the call must be treated as an asset and the item must cease to be treated as an item of _ancillary own funds_.

**[Note: Art. 89(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.5

When determining its _own funds_ , a _firm_ must not take into account any item of _ancillary own funds_ unless, subject to [2.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#946e2318b29646c895fa2b0e2ba5481b "2.6"), it has received an ancillary own funds permission in respect of that item specifying either:

  1. (1) a monetary amount for the relevant item of _ancillary own funds_ ; or
  2. (2) the method by which to determine the amount of the relevant item of _ancillary own funds_ , together with the amount determined in accordance with that method for a specified time period.

**[Note: Art. 90(1) and (3) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

2.6

Where, in respect of an ancillary own funds item, a firm has received an ancillary own funds permission:

  1. (1) that specifies a monetary amount, in accordance with[ 2.5(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#12a873b504854ba685cc8615b25767f1), the firm may only include that item in its _own funds_ up to the monetary amount set out in the ancillary own funds permission; or
  2. (2) that specifies a method by which to determine a monetary amount in accordance with [2.5(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#12a873b504854ba685cc8615b25767f1), the firm may only include that item in its _own funds_ up to the monetary amount that has been determined by the method set out in, and only for the time period specified by, the ancillary own funds permission.

**[Note: Art. 90(3) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

2.7

A _firm_ may only attribute an amount to an item of _ancillary own funds_ to the extent that it:

  1. (1) reflects the loss-absorbency of the item; and
  2. (2) is based upon prudent and realistic assumptions.

**[Note: Art. 90(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Classification of Own Funds Into Tiers

3.1

A _firm_ may only include an _own funds_ item in its _Tier 1 own funds_ if:

  1. (1) it is an item of _basic own funds_ ; and
  2. (2) it substantially possesses the characteristics set out in [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5")(1) and [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5")(2), taking into consideration the features set out in [3.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#c53baf7efd9149f0a286ce2d2b7906a8 "3.6").

**[Note: Art. 94(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.2

A _firm_ may only include an _own funds_ item in its _Tier 2 own funds_ if:

  1. (1) where it is an item of _basic own funds_ , it substantially possesses the characteristics set out in [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5")(2), taking into consideration the features set out in [3.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#c53baf7efd9149f0a286ce2d2b7906a8 "3.6"); or
  2. (2) where it is an item of _ancillary own funds_ , it substantially possesses the characteristics set out in [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5")(1) and [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5")(2), taking into consideration the features set out in [3.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#c53baf7efd9149f0a286ce2d2b7906a8 "3.6").

**[Note: Art. 94(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.3

A _firm_ may only include in its _Tier 3 own funds_ an item of:

  1. (1) _basic own funds_ that does not fall within [3.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#8185b18ba3694a31b626911a1d6d4a33 "3.1") or [3.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#da6b4bf136c14d6b809ece8c02fbc26b "3.2")(1); and
  2. (2) _ancillary own funds_ that does not fall within [3.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#da6b4bf136c14d6b809ece8c02fbc26b "3.2")(2).

**[Note: Art. 94(3) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.4

  1. (1) In classifying its _own funds_ items, a _firm_ must refer to the __own funds_ lists._
  2. (2) A _firm_ must not include an _own funds_ item in its _Tier 1 own funds_ , _Tier 2 own funds_ or _Tier 3 own funds_ if that _own funds_ item is not covered by the  __own funds_ lists_, unless it has received a classification of own funds permission in respect of that item.
  3. (3) [Deleted]

**[Note: Art. 95 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.4A

Notwithstanding that a firm has received a classification of own funds permission, basic own funds items not included in the own funds lists may only be classified as Tier 1 own funds where they are fully paid in.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.5

The characteristics referred to in [3.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#8185b18ba3694a31b626911a1d6d4a33 "3.1")(2) and [3.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#da6b4bf136c14d6b809ece8c02fbc26b "3.2") are:

  1. (1) the item is available, or can be called up on demand, to fully absorb losses on a going-concern basis as well as in the case of winding up (permanent availability); and
  2. (2) in the case of winding up, the total amount of the item is available to absorb losses and the repayment of the item is refused to its holder until all other obligations, including insurance and _reinsurance_ obligations to _policyholders_ , have been met (subordination).

**[Note: Art. 93(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.6

When assessing the extent to which _own funds_ items possess the characteristics set out in [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388 "3.5"), currently and in the future, a _firm_ must consider:

  1. (1) the duration of the item, in particular whether the item is dated or not and, where an _own funds_ item is dated, the relative duration of the item as compared to the duration of the insurance and _reinsurance_ obligations of the _firm_ (sufficient duration);
  2. (2) whether the item is free from requirements or incentives to redeem the nominal sum (absence of incentives to redeem);
  3. (3) whether the item is free from mandatory fixed charges (absence of mandatory servicing costs); and
  4. (4) whether the item is clear of encumbrances (absence of encumbrances).

**[Note: Art. 93(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.7

  1. (1) A firm must not classify as Tier 1 own funds:
     1. (a) paid-in ordinary share capital and related share premium account; or
     2. (b) paid-in initial fund, member’s contribution or the equivalent basic own funds for a mutual

unless the firm has the right to cancel and withhold dividends or other distributions in respect of those items at any time prior to payment, (and exercises that right) in the circumstances specified in [3B.1(12)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734).

  1. (2) A firm must not classify as Tier 2 basic own funds:
     1. (a) ordinary share capital and related share premium account; or
     2. (b) initial fund, member’s contribution or the equivalent basic own funds for a mutual

unless the firm has the right to defer dividends or other distributions in respect of those items at any time prior to payment, (and exercises that right) in the circumstances specified in [3E.1(8)](/pra-rules/own-funds#bdd622c98aa848f689b5c9226e3c7541).

**[Note: Art. 93 and Art. 94 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3A

###  Tier 1 – List of Own Funds Items

3A.1

The following basic own funds items shall be deemed to substantially possess the characteristics set out in [3.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#4281390817d244869122d31ba2562388), taking into consideration the features set out in [3.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#c53baf7efd9149f0a286ce2d2b7906a8), and a firm must classify them as Tier 1 own funds, where the following items display all of the features set out in [3B](/pra-rules/own-funds#6c8b094152ef42f1a89c3f757ee3395f):

  1. (1) the part of excess of assets over liabilities, valued in accordance with [2](/pra-rules/valuation#b1dd1f10c578445ebef4a906b3c2aee9) of the [Valuation Part](/pra-rules/valuation) and [Technical Provisions Part](/pra-rules/technical-provisions), comprising the following items:
     1. (a) paid-in ordinary share capital and the related share premium account;
     2. (b) paid-in initial funds, members’ contributions or the equivalent basic own funds item for mutual and mutual-type undertakings;
     3. (c) paid-in subordinated mutual member accounts;
     4. (d) surplus funds that are not considered as insurance and reinsurance obligations in accordance with [2.1](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#87926c96662148b98c378f06d0414fec) of the [Surplus Funds Part](/pra-rules/surplus-funds);
     5. (e) paid-in preference shares and the related share premium account; and
     6. (f) a reconciliation reserve calculated in accordance with [3C](/pra-rules/own-funds#22431b9fc8fb4636bfc04088122915d6);
  2. (2) paid-in subordinated liabilities valued in accordance with [2](/pra-rules/valuation#b1dd1f10c578445ebef4a906b3c2aee9) of the [Valuation Part](/pra-rules/valuation).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

  1. (1) the basic own funds item:
     1. (a) in the case of items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) and [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), ranks after all other claims in the event of winding-up proceedings regarding the firm; and

(5) the basic own funds item, in the case of restricted Tier 1 own funds, possesses one of the following principal loss absorbency mechanisms to be triggered at the trigger event specified in [3B.10](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) and complies with the conditions set out in [3B.9](/pra-rules/own-funds#a04be115d4634578abe6b50dcb184ff5):
     1. (a) the nominal or principal amount of the basic own funds item is written down as set out in [3B.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#844c81e029ce46159f35daddb40bfa21) and [3B.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#c06c7e656678409287d8738b32291b89);

(b) the basic own funds item automatically converts into a basic own funds item listed in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) or [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) as set out in [3B.7](/pra-rules/own-funds#4584f90fae6149ca852d49af1b468288) and [3B.8](/pra-rules/own-funds#cc15d150b8584b4d98cb39cecaec0f5d); or
     3. (c) a principal loss absorbency mechanism that achieves an equivalent outcome to the principal loss absorbency mechanisms set out in [3B.1(5)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734) or [3B.1(5)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734);
  6. (6) the basic own funds item meets one of the following criteria:
     1. (a) in the case of items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) and [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), the item is undated or, where the firm has a fixed maturity, is of the same maturity as the firm; or

(7) a restricted Tier 1 own funds item may only allow for repayment or redemption of that item between 5 and 10 years after the date of issuance where the firm’s SCR is exceeded by an appropriate margin taking into account the solvency position of the firm including the firm’s medium-term capital management plan;

(8) the basic own funds item, in the case of items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715), [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(c)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(e)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715), and [3A.1(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), is only repayable or redeemable at the option of the firm and provides that the repayment or redemption of the basic own funds item is subject to the firm receiving prior permission from the PRA;

     1. (a)  the firm complies with the SCR; and
     2. (b) the repayment or redemption would not lead to non-compliance with the SCR,
     3. other than in the circumstances set out in [3B.1(11)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734);
  11. (11) notwithstanding [3B.1(10)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), the basic own funds item may allow for repayment or redemption of that item where the firm does not comply with the SCR or repayment or redemption would lead to such non-compliance, only where all of the following conditions are met:

     2. (b) the item is to be exchanged for or converted into another Tier 1 own funds item of at least the same quality; and
     3. (c) the MCR will be complied with after the repayment or redemption;
  12. (12) the basic own funds item meets one of the following criteria:
     1. (a) in the case of items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) and [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), either the legal or contractual arrangements governing the basic own funds item or legislation applicable in the UK allow for the cancellation of distributions in relation to that item where there is non-compliance with the SCR or the distribution would lead to such non-compliance until:
        1. (i) the firm complies with the SCR; and
        2. (ii) the distribution would not lead to non-compliance with the SCR,
        3. other than in the circumstances set out in [3B.1(13)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734);
     2. (b) in the case of restricted Tier 1 own funds items, the terms of the contractual arrangement governing the basic own funds item provide for the cancellation of distributions in relation to that item where there is non-compliance with the SCR or the distribution would lead to such non-compliance until:
        1. (i) the firm complies with the SCR; and
        2. (ii) the distribution would not lead to non-compliance with the SCR,

     1. (a) the firm has received prior permission from the PRA that the distribution can be made;
     2. (b) the distribution would not further weaken the solvency position of the firm; and
     3. (c) the MCR will be complied with after the distribution is made;
  14. (14) the basic own funds item, in the case of items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715), [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(c)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(e)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715), and [3A.1(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), provides the firm with full flexibility over the distributions on the basic own funds item in accordance with the conditions set out in [3B.3](/pra-rules/own-funds#a64e58b6f9c042dea4fda11ef49b963b) or [3B.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#97e809a13d524b66bacaff5a5f0be552); and
  15. (15) the basic own funds item is free from encumbrances and is not connected with any other transaction, which when considered with the basic own funds item, could result in that basic own funds item not complying with [3.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#8185b18ba3694a31b626911a1d6d4a33).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.2

For the purposes of [3B](/pra-rules/own-funds#6c8b094152ef42f1a89c3f757ee3395f), the exchange or conversion of a basic own funds item into another Tier 1 own funds item or the repayment or redemption of a Tier 1 own funds item out of the proceeds of a new basic own funds item of at least the same quality will not be deemed to be a repayment or redemption, provided that the exchange, conversion, repayment or redemption is subject to receiving prior permission from the PRA.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.3

For the purposes of [3B.1(14)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), in the case of basic own funds items referred to in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) and [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), the item provides full flexibility over distributions only where all of the following conditions are met:

  1. (1) there is no preferential distribution treatment regarding the order of distribution payments and the terms of the contractual arrangement governing the own funds item do not provide preferential rights to the payment of distributions;
  2. (2) distributions are paid out of distributable items;
  3. (3) the level of distributions is not determined on the basis of the amount for which the own funds item was purchased at issuance and there is no cap or other restriction on the maximum level of distribution;
  4. (4) notwithstanding [3B.3(3)](/pra-rules/own-funds#a64e58b6f9c042dea4fda11ef49b963b), in the case of instruments issued by mutual and mutual-type undertakings, a cap or other restriction on the maximum level of distribution may be set, provided that cap or other restriction is not an event linked to distributions being made, or not made, on other own funds items;
  5. (5) there is no obligation for a firm to make distributions;
  6. (6) non-payment of distributions does not constitute an event of default by the firm; and
  7. (7) the cancellation of distributions imposes no restrictions on the firm.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.4

For the purposes of [3B.1(14)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), in the case of restricted Tier 1 own funds items, the item provides full flexibility over distributions only where all of the following conditions are met:

  1. (1) distributions are paid out of distributable items;
  2. (2) the firm has full discretion at all times to cancel distributions in relation to the own funds item for an unlimited period and on a non-cumulative basis and the firm may use the cancelled payments without restriction to meet its obligations as they fall due;
  3. (3) there is no obligation to substitute the distribution by a payment in any other form;
  4. (4) there is no obligation to make distributions in the event of a distribution being made on another own funds item;
  5. (5) non-payment of distributions does not constitute an event of default by the firm; and
  6. (6) the cancellation of distributions imposes no restrictions on the firm.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

For the purposes of [3B.1(5)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), the nominal or principal amount of the basic own funds item must be written down in such a way that all of the following are reduced:

For the purposes of [3B.1(5)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), the provisions governing the write-down of the nominal or principal amount of the basic own funds item must provide for all of the following:

  1. (1) if the trigger event specified in [3B.10](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) has occurred in the circumstances described in [3B.10(3)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) and a partial write-down would be sufficient to re-establish compliance with the SCR, there is a partial write-down of the nominal or principal amount for an amount that is at least sufficient to re-establish compliance with the SCR;
  2. (2) if the trigger event specified in [3B.10](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) has occurred in the circumstances described in [3B.10(3)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) and a partial write-down would not be sufficient to re-establish compliance with the SCR, the nominal or principal amount as determined at the time of original issuance of the basic own funds item is written down at least on a linear basis in a manner which ensures that full write-down will occur when 75% coverage of the SCR is reached, or prior to that event;
  3. (3) if the trigger event specified in [3B.10](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) has occurred in the circumstances described in [3B.10(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) or [3B.10(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309), the nominal or principal amount is written down in full; and
  4. (4) following a write-down in accordance with [3B.6(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c06c7e656678409287d8738b32291b89) (‘the initial write-down’):
     1. (a) if the trigger event specified in [3B.10](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) subsequently occurs in the circumstances described in [3B.10(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) or [3B.10(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309), the nominal or principal amount is written down in full;
     2. (b) if, by the end of the period of three months from the date of the trigger event that resulted in the initial write-down, no trigger event has occurred in the circumstances described in [3B.10(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) or [3B.10(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) but the solvency ratio has deteriorated further, the nominal or principal amount as determined at the time of original issuance of the basic own funds item is written down further in accordance with [3B.6(2)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c06c7e656678409287d8738b32291b89) to reflect that further deterioration in the solvency ratio; and
     3. (c) a further write-down is made in accordance with [3B.6(4)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c06c7e656678409287d8738b32291b89) for each subsequent deterioration in the solvency ratio at the end of each subsequent period of three months until the firm has re-established compliance with the SCR.

For the purposes of [3B.6(4)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c06c7e656678409287d8738b32291b89), the ‘solvency ratio’ means the ratio of eligible own funds (to cover a firm’s SCR) and the firm’s SCR using the latest available values.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

For the purposes of [3B.1(5)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734), the provisions governing the conversion into basic own funds items listed in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) or [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) must specify either of the following:

3B.9

The nominal or principal amount of the basic own funds item must absorb losses at the trigger event. Loss absorbency resulting from the cancellation of, or a reduction in, distributions does not constitute a principal loss absorbency mechanism in accordance with [3B.1(5)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.10

The trigger event referred to in [3B.1(5)](/pra-rules/own-funds#7df6eecb1dd140deb4836489809ce734) is significant non-compliance with the SCR. Non-compliance with the SCR is considered significant for these purposes where any of the following conditions is met:

  1. (1) the amount of own funds items eligible to cover the SCR is equal to or less than 75% of the SCR;
  2. (2) the amount of own funds items eligible to cover the MCR is equal to or less than MCR; or
  3. (3) compliance with the SCR is not re-established within a period of three months from the date when non-compliance with the SCR was first observed.

A firm may specify, in the provisions governing the instrument, one or more trigger events in addition to the events referred to in [3B.10(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309) to [3B.10(3)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

  1. (1) the trigger event occurs in the circumstances described in [3B.10(3)](/pra-rules/own-funds#70af39f323394375925492ed32adc309);

  3. (3) the firm has received prior permission from the PRA to waive the triggering of the principal loss absorbency mechanism on the basis of the following information:
     1. (a) projections provided to the PRA by the firm when that firm submits the recovery plan required by [4.4(2)](/pra-rules/group-supervision#5c3352db90a747f2856b90c7c0bbfaa5) of the [Group Supervision Part](/pra-rules/group-supervision) and [3.1(2)](/pra-rules/undertakings-in-difficulty#bb1ede0d101246348f322b247064ad23) of the [Undertakings in Difficulty Part](/pra-rules/undertakings-in-difficulty), demonstrate that triggering the principal loss absorbency mechanism in that case would be very likely to give rise to a tax liability that would have a significant adverse effect on the firm’s solvency position; and
     2. (b)  a certificate issued by the firm’s statutory auditors certifying that all of the assumptions used in the projections are realistic.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

###  Reconciliation Reserve

3C.1

The reconciliation reserve referred to in [3A.1(1)(f)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c0c0926aa5f141c19acb6f33fef2a715) equals the total excess of assets over liabilities reduced by all of the following:

  1. (1) the amount of own shares held by the firm;
  2. (2) foreseeable dividends, distributions and charges;
  3. (3) the basic own funds items included in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) to [(e)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3D.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#206deb6059c3406d83f8478dc25c9a8c) and [3F.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#cc0225d4d49248ed8dd33a1e18f734f5);
  4. (4) any basic own funds item not included in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) to [(e)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3D.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#206deb6059c3406d83f8478dc25c9a8c) [3F.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#cc0225d4d49248ed8dd33a1e18f734f5), in respect of which a firm has a classification of own funds permission;
  5. (5) the restricted own funds items that meet one of the following requirements:
     1. (a) exceed the notional SCR in the case of matching adjustment portfolios and ring-fenced funds determined in accordance with [3L.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#4f4ea099bb0d4198ac050433984bf298); or
     2. (b) that are excluded in accordance with [3L.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#3effdaff54d44d01adfd95aeb18bdfcf); and
  6. (6) the amount of participations held in financial and credit institutions as referred to in [3K.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#fe2738c2e38f430d8f7e032503d00a8c) deducted in accordance with [3K](/pra-rules/own-funds#fe2925d055154887803b51ac2cb4177f), to the extent that this is not already included in [3C.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2d00cca5211f477d83988b467406b784) to [(5)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2d00cca5211f477d83988b467406b784).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3C.2

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3C.3

For the purpose of determining its basic own funds, a firm must reduce its basic own funds by the part of the value of all participations, as referred to in [3K.6](/pra-rules/own-funds#fe2738c2e38f430d8f7e032503d00a8c), in financial and credit institutions, other than participations referred to in [3K.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#83f4eb2057a74cf4bb49376fe316513c), to the extent the aggregate value exceeds 10% of items included in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(d)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) and [3A.1(1)(f)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3K.3

Notwithstanding [3K.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#83f4eb2057a74cf4bb49376fe316513c) and [3K.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a39f16fc9104563be1a10f24244d557), a firm must not deduct strategic participations as referred to in [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula 3D10](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#111eba65da7e49488fd3ebe6d6905679) which are included in the calculation of the group solvency on the basis of method 1 as set out in the [Financial Conglomerates Part](/pra-rules/financial-conglomerates) or on the basis of method 1 as set out in [11](/pra-rules/group-supervision#deb969a774e84eda87f96d00262dcfc4) of the [Group Supervision Part](/pra-rules/group-supervision).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3K.4

A firm must apply the deductions set out in [3K.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a39f16fc9104563be1a10f24244d557) on a pro-rata basis to all participations to which [3K.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a39f16fc9104563be1a10f24244d557) refers.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3K.5

A firm must make the deductions set out in [3K.1](/pra-rules/own-funds#83f4eb2057a74cf4bb49376fe316513c) and [3K.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a39f16fc9104563be1a10f24244d557) from the corresponding tier in which the participation has increased the own funds of the related undertaking as follows:

  1. (1) holdings of Common Equity Tier 1 items of financial and credit institutions must be deducted from the items included in [3A.1(1)(a)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(b)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0), [3A.1(1)(d)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0) and [3A.1(1)(f)](/pra-rules/own-funds#2a294d7b204b4532a9bd9747a854bef0);
  2. (2) holdings of Additional Tier 1 instruments of financial and credit institutions must be deducted from the Tier 1 own funds items that display the features set out in [3B](/pra-rules/own-funds#6c8b094152ef42f1a89c3f757ee3395f) that are relevant for restricted Tier 1 own funds items; and
  3. (3) holdings of Tier 2 instruments of financial and credit institutions must be deducted from the basic own funds items included in [3D](/pra-rules/own-funds#56b33772e89b41bdb74c9b3c5a1f3803).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3K.6

Participations in financial and credit institutions must comprise the following:

  1. (1) participations which the firm holds in:
     1. (a) credit institutions and financial institutions;
     2. (b) investment firms; and
  2. (2) subordinated claims and instruments referred to in Articles 61 to 63 of CRR which a firm holds in respect of the entities defined in [3K.6(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#fe2738c2e38f430d8f7e032503d00a8c) in which it holds a participation.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3L

###  Adjustment for Ring-Fenced Funds and Matching Adjustment Portfolios

3L.1

Subject to [3L.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#3effdaff54d44d01adfd95aeb18bdfcf), for the purposes of calculating the reconciliation reserve, a firm must reduce the excess of assets over liabilities referred to in [3C](/pra-rules/own-funds#22431b9fc8fb4636bfc04088122915d6) by the amount of restricted own funds items within a ring-fenced fund or matching adjustment portfolio in excess of the notional SCR of the ring-fenced fund or matching adjustment portfolio.

Where the firm calculates the SCR using the standard formula, it must calculate the notional SCR of a ring-fenced fund or matching adjustment portfolio in accordance with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula 9.1](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#ef38f3a2df234560b3ec1e6f7fd25148).

Where the firm calculates the SCR using an internal model, it must calculate the notional SCR of a ring-fenced fund or matching adjustment portfolio using that internal model, as if the firm pursued only the business included in the ring-fenced fund or matching adjustment portfolio.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3L.2

Where the assets, the liabilities and the risk within a ring-fenced fund are not material, a firm may reduce the reconciliation reserve by the total amount of restricted own funds items.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

4

###  Eligibility and Limits Applicable to Tiers

4.1

As far as compliance with its _SCR_ is concerned at least the following conditions must be met:

  1. (1) more than one-third of the total amount of the _firm’s_ _eligible own funds_ is accounted for by _Tier 1 own funds_ ; and
  2. (2) less than one-third of the _firm’s eligible own funds_ is accounted for by _Tier 3 own funds_.

**[Note: Art. 98(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.2

As far as compliance with its _MCR_ is concerned, as a minimum more than 50% of the _firm’s eligible own funds_ must be accounted for by _Tier 1 own funds_.

**[Note: Art. 98(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4A

###  Eligibility and Limits Applicable to Tiers 1, 2 and 3

4A.1

As far as compliance with the SCR is concerned, a firm must ensure that:

  1. (1) Tier 1 own funds items account for at least 50% of the SCR;
  2. (2) Tier 3 own funds items account for less than 15% of the SCR; and
  3. (3) the sum of the amounts of Tier 2 own funds items and Tier 3 own funds items do not account for more than 50% of the SCR.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

4A.2

As far as compliance with the MCR is concerned, a firm must ensure that:

  1. (1) Tier 1 own funds items account for at least 80% of the MCR; and
  2. (2) Tier 2 own funds items do not account for more than 20% of the MCR.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

4A.3

For the purposes of [4A.1(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#b5eff8532e124886bcefacaad96d762e) and[ 4A.2(1)](/pra-rules/own-funds#c1c9ef21871c4c87af1d3d8559221f8a), the sum of the following basic own funds items must make up less than 20% of the total amount of Tier 1 own funds items:

  1. (1) Tier 1 own funds items that display the features set out in [3B](/pra-rules/own-funds#6c8b094152ef42f1a89c3f757ee3395f) that are relevant for restricted Tier 1 own funds items; and
  2. (2) items that are included in Tier 1 own funds under the transitional arrangement set out in [Transitional Measures 4.1](/pra-rules/transitional-measures#00777985d4084b339908d98980094139).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

4A.4

A firm must apply the quantitative limits set out in [4A](/pra-rules/own-funds#4f66d9116da34c6a890bcbbfb67d8f35) to own funds items in respect of which it has received a classification of own funds permission.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

5

###  Notification of Issuance of Own Funds Items

5.1

This Chapter does not apply in respect of the following:

  1. (1) any item which a _firm_ intends to include within its _basic own funds_ that is not included in the own funds lists, but in respect of which the _firm_ would need to receive a classification of own funds permission; and
  2. (2) any item which a _firm_ in respect of which a firm would need to receive an ancillary own funds permission.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

5.2

  1. (1) Subject to [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5"), a _firm_ must notify the _PRA_ in writing of its intention to issue an item which it intends to include within its _basic own funds_ at least one _month_ before the intended date of issue, unless there are exceptional circumstances which make it impracticable to give such a period of notice. In such circumstances, the _firm_ must give the _PRA_ as much notice as is practicable in those circumstances and explain to the _PRA_ why the circumstances are considered exceptional.
  2. (2) When giving notice, a _firm_ must:
     1. (a) provide details of the amount of _basic own funds_ the _firm_ is seeking to raise through the intended issue and whether the item is intended to be issued to external investors or within its _group_ ;
     2. (b) identify the classification of _basic own funds_ the item is intended to fall within;
     3. (c) provide a copy of the draft terms and conditions;
     4. (d) provide a draft of a properly reasoned independent legal opinion from an appropriately qualified individual confirming that the item complies with the rules applicable to items of _basic own funds_ included in the classification of the item identified in (b);
     5. (e) for any item referred to in [4A.3](/pra-rules/own-funds#76c52b100e284241b4bd41d8baebf025), provide a draft of a properly reasoned independent accounting opinion from an appropriately qualified individual as to the item’s treatment in the _firm’s_ financial statements;
     6. (f) include confirmation from the _governing body_ of the _firm_ that the item complies with the rules applicable to items of _basic own funds_ included in the classification of the item identified in (b); and
     7. (g) state whether the item is encumbered or whether there are any connected transactions in respect of the item and, if so, provide details.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

5.3

If after an initial notification under [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2"), but prior to an item’s issuance, a _firm_ proposes to change the information previously submitted, it must provide a further written notification of that change without delay.

*Effective: 01/03/2016*

5.4

If a _firm_ proposes to establish or amend a debt securities programme for the issue of an item for inclusion within its _basic own funds_ , it must:

  1. (1) notify the _PRA_ of the establishment of the programme or of the proposed amendment to the programme; and
  2. (2) provide the information required by [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)

at least one _month_ before any proposed drawdown. The _PRA_ must be notified of any changes in accordance with [5.3](/pra-rules/own-funds#a01da3bd1f5848069d71b8b2a802b9c8 "5.3").

*Effective: 01/03/2017*

5.5

[5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2") does not apply to:

  1. (1) ordinary _shares_ which:
     1. (a) meet the classification criteria for ordinary share capital in _Tier 1 own funds;_ and
     2. (b) are the same as ordinary _shares_ previously issued by the _firm_ ;
  2. (2) debt instruments issued from a debt securities programme, provided that:
  3.      1. (a) the establishment of (and any subsequent amendment to) the programme was notified to the _PRA_ in accordance with [5.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#3d516a830ed744a081135edb64bac60c "5.4") and the last such notification was given to the _PRA_ no more than twelve months prior to the date of the proposed drawdown;
     2. (b) the programme complies with, and the information previously notified to the _PRA_ in accordance with [5.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#3d516a830ed744a081135edb64bac60c "5.4") in relation to the programme is unaffected by, any changes in law or regulation, or the interpretation or application of either, coming into effect since the last notification in accordance with [5.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#3d516a830ed744a081135edb64bac60c "5.4"); and
     3. (c) any instrument issued pursuant to the programme must, under the terms of the programme, constitute _basic own funds_ ; and
  4. (3) any item which is to be issued on identical terms to one or more items included in _basic own funds_ issued by the _firm_ within the previous twelve months and notified to the PRA in accordance with [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2"), excluding (1) the issue date, (2) the maturity date, (3) the amount of the issuance, (4) the currency of the issuance, and (5) the rate of interest payable by the issuer.

*Effective: 01/03/2017*

5.6

A _firm_ must notify the _PRA_ in writing, no later than the date of issue, of its intention to issue an item listed in [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5") which it intends to include within its _basic own funds_. When giving notice, a _firm_ must:

  1. (1) provide the information set out in [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2") other than [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)(c) (draft terms and conditions), [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)(d) (draft legal opinion) and [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)(e) (draft accounting opinion); and
  2. (2) for issuance of an item pursuant to [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5")(1) or [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5")(3), confirm that the terms of the item have not changed since the previous issue by the _firm_ of that type of item of _basic own funds_.

*Effective: 01/03/2017*

5.7

A _firm_ must notify the _PRA_ in writing of its intention to amend or otherwise vary the terms of any item included within its _basic own funds_ at least one _month_ before the intended date of such amendment or other variation.

*Effective: 01/03/2016*

5.8

A firm must provide to the _PRA_ as soon as practicable after the issuance of an item of _basic own funds_ to which [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2"), [5.4](/pra-rules/own-funds#3d516a830ed744a081135edb64bac60c "5.4"), [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5")(2) or [5.5](/pra-rules/own-funds#afa09ce4724c4106af566f8217370236 "5.5")(3) applies:

  1. (1) a finalised copy of the draft legal opinion referred to in [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)(d);
  2. (2) a finalised copy of the draft accounting opinion referred to in [5.2](/pra-rules/own-funds#a4c51ee85a9a414dbd0cc5d4b58e5bfc "5.2")(2)(e) if applicable;
  3. (3) a copy of the instrument’s final terms and conditions; and
  4. (4) a reasoned basis for the choice of coupon structure and any other provision that might suggest an incentive to redeem.

*Effective: 02/05/2017*

###  Lloyd’s

6.1

This Chapter applies to the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.2

For the purposes of complying with the _SCR Rules_ , the _Society_ must categorise _own funds_ at Lloyd’s as between:

  1. (1) _own funds_ attributable to the _Society_ ; and
  2. (2) _own funds_ attributable to _members_ which are available to support _members’_ insurance business at Lloyd’s, including _funds at Lloyd’s_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

The _Society_ must notify the _PRA_ in writing, within 14 days, in the event the _Council_ makes a determination pursuant to paragraph 8(1A) or varies a determination pursuant to paragraph 8(1B)(a) of the New Central Fund Byelaw (No 23 of 1996) as amended, that the _Central Fund_ or a part of the _Central Fund_ is to constitute _own funds_ attributable to the _Society_ for the purposes of covering the _SCR_ for Lloyd’s.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.4

In determining _own funds_ at Lloyd’s in accordance with [2](/pra-rules/own-funds#830bf1efa938482d9d2fc4e8d1a81a7d "2"), the _Society_ shall have regard to:

  1. (1) the _Society’s_ _central assets_ and _central liabilities_ ; and
  2. (2) the assets and liabilities of _members_ , including assets which are available to support _members’_ _insurance business_ at Lloyd’s, such assets including a _members’_ _funds at Lloyd’s_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
