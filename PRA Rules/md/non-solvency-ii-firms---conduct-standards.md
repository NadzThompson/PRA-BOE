# Non-Solvency II Firms - Conduct Standards

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

This Part applies:

  1. (1) to a _small non-directive insurer_ ; and
  2. (2) in relation to a _small non-directive insurer_ , to any _person_ who is:
     1. (a) a _Conduct Rules non-executive director_ ;
     2. (b) approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
        1. (i) the _PRA_ ; or
        2. (ii) the _FCA_ , in relation to a relevant senior management function;
     3. (c) a _certification employee_ ;
     4. (d) an _employee_ who should have been approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
        1. (i) the _PRA_ ; or
        2. (ii) the _FCA_ , in relation to a relevant senior management function; or
     5. (e) an _employee_ who is performing a function that would have been a _controlled function_ but for [Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Management Functions 2.3](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---senior-management-functions#b324ce6a53024b6db0056695b55eed5d "Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Management Functions 2.3").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.2

In this Part the following definition shall apply:

_employee_

has the meaning given in [Non-solvency II Firms – Certification 1.2](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#d729dc37c48f4ec1a308dde7c7d1be7b "Non-solvency II Firms – Certification 1.2").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1A

###  Scope of Conduct Standards

1A.1

If you are a natural person who is approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:

  1. (1) the _PRA_ ; or
  2. (2) the _FCA_ , in relation to a _relevant senior management function_ ,

you must comply at all times with all of the _conduct standards_.

*Effective: 03/07/2017*

1A.2

If you are a _Conduct Rules non-executive director_ , you must comply at all times with the _conduct standards_ specified in [2.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#ff997b9e4b104ef8a64809e7115e786e "2.1") to [2.3](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#2de16f8f97d1497f9cf5b0cff876eae4 "2.3"), [2.7](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#1801cea6da834acaa42c20f578ae83a5 "2.7") and [2.8](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#9aa0b3f13fcf46cf8acc84dabb0d09e6 "2.8").

*Effective: 03/07/2017*

1A.3

If you are an _employee_ of a type specified in [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(c), [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(d) or [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(e) you must comply at all times with the _conduct standards_ specified in [2.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#ff997b9e4b104ef8a64809e7115e786e "2.1") to to [2.3](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#2de16f8f97d1497f9cf5b0cff876eae4 "2.3").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1A.4

A _firm_ must require:

  1. (1) a natural _person_ of a type specified in [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(b) to observe all of the _conduct standards_ ; and
  2. (2) an _employee_ of a type specified in [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(c), [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(d) or [1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "1.1")(2)(e) to observe the _conduct standards_ specified in [2.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#ff997b9e4b104ef8a64809e7115e786e "2.1") to [2.3](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#2de16f8f97d1497f9cf5b0cff876eae4 "2.3").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Conduct Standards

2.1

Conduct Standard 1: You must act with integrity.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.2

Conduct Standard 2: You must act with due skill, care and diligence.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.3

Conduct Standard 3: You must be open and co-operative with the _FCA_ , the _PRA_ and other regulators.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.4

Conduct Standard 4: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible is controlled effectively.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.5

Conduct Standard 5: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible complies with the relevant requirements and standards of the _regulatory system_.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.6

Conduct Standard 6: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that any delegation of your responsibilities is to an appropriate _person_ and that you oversee the discharge of the delegated responsibility effectively.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.7

Conduct Standard 7: You must disclose appropriately any information of which the _FCA_ or the _PRA_ would reasonably expect to have notice.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.8

Conduct Standard 8: When exercising your responsibilities, you must pay due regard to the interests of current and potential future _policyholders_ in ensuring the provision by the _firm_ of an appropriate degree of protection for their insured benefits.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*
