# Non-Solvency II Firms - Run-off Firms

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _run-off firm_ ; and
  2. (2) in relation to a ___run-off firm_ , any _person_ who is approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
     1. (a) the _PRA_ ; or
     2. (b) the _FCA_ , in relation to a _relevant senior management functio_ n.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_application rules_

means the specifications within:

     1. (1) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Management Functions 1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---senior-management-functions#69d36e085b624130b8b453028b71958d "Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Insurance Management Functions 1.1");
     2. (2) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Fitness and Propriety 1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---fitness-and-propriety#b5dda0aa9e6a490ebfa619236803f064 "Non-Solvency II Firms - Fitness and Propriety 1.1");
     3. (3) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Allocation of Responsibilities 1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---allocation-of-responsibilities#9c062cacca554d3bacdf961eeea69072 "Non-Solvency II Firms - Allocation of Responsibilities 1.1"); and
     4. (4) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms-senior-managers-regime-applications-and-notifications#67c4650ea4d641e4b541254e8206dd09 "Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Insurance Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 1.1").

_run-off firm_

means a _firm_ to which, but for the application of [Transitional Measures 2.1](/pra-rules/transitional-measures#ce01c09e148b43cda20c93d1e5e68a59 "Transitional Measures 2.1") to [2.3](/pra-rules/transitional-measures#69745ab3af33416894f8f44f0597f027 "2.3"), the _Solvency II rules_ would apply.

_Solvency II rules_

means the _PRA_ rules in the [Valuation](/pra-rules/valuation "Valuation"), [Technical Provisions](/pra-rules/technical-provisions "Technical Provisions"), [Own Funds](/pra-rules/own-funds "Own Funds"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – General Provisions](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions "Solvency Capital Requirement – General Provisions"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula "Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models "Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models"), [Minimum Capital Requirement](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement "Minimum Capital Requirement"), [Undertakings in Difficulty](/pra-rules/undertakings-in-difficulty "Undertakings in Difficulty"), [Investments](/pra-rules/investments "Investments"), [Composites](/pra-rules/composites "Composites"), [Conditions Governing Business](/pra-rules/conditions-governing-business "Conditions Governing Business"), [Insurance Special Purpose Vehicles](/pra-rules/insurance-special-purpose-vehicles "Insurance Special Purpose Vehicles"), [Group Supervision](/pra-rules/group-supervision "Group Supervision"), [Reporting](/pra-rules/reporting "Reporting"), [Surplus Funds](/pra-rules/surplus-funds "Surplus Funds"), [With-Profits](/pra-rules/with-profits "With-Profits"), [Insurance – Conduct Standards](/pra-rules/insurance---conduct-standards "Insurance – Conduct Standards"), [Insurance – Senior Management Functions](/pra-rules/insurance---senior-management-functions "Insurance – Senior Insurance Management Functions"), [Insurance – Allocation of Responsibilities](/pra-rules/insurance---allocation-of-responsibilities "Insurance – Allocation of Responsibilities") and [Insurance – Fitness and Propriety](/pra-rules/insurance---fitness-and-propriety "Insurance – Fitness and Propriety") Parts of the [Solvency II Firms](/pra-rules/sii-firms "Solvency II Firms") Sector of the _PRA_ Rulebook.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Transitional Application of Rules in Relation to Run-Off Firms

2.1

Notwithstanding the _application rules_ , the following Parts apply to a _run-off firm_ for so long as it remains a _run-off firm_ :

  1. (1) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Management Functions](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---senior-management-functions "Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Insurance Management Functions");
  2. (2) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Fitness and Propriety;](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---fitness-and-propriety "Non-Solvency II Firms - Fitness and Propriety;")
  3. (3) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Allocation of Responsibilities](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---allocation-of-responsibilities "Non-Solvency II Firms - Allocation of Responsibilities"); and
  4. (4) [Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms-senior-managers-regime-applications-and-notifications "Non-Solvency II Firms - Senior Insurance Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

Notwithstanding [Non-Solvency II Firms - Conduct Standards 1.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards#f4dbc1b2e8e4437e9475d6e7b06425cc "Non-Solvency II Firms - Conduct Standards 1.1"), the rules in [Non-Solvency II Firms - Conduct Standards](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms---conduct-standards "Non-Solvency II Firms - Conduct Standards") apply to any _person_ subject to this Part.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*
