# Leverage Ratio – Capital Requirements and Buffers

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) every _CRR firm_ that:
     1. (a1) has  _retail deposits_ equal to or greater than £75 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1B](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#8acf2f62eb084d28a585b3bc98f97be8), on an individual basis; or
     2. (a2) that has _foreign assets_ equal to or greater £10 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1A](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#d1cccea121134ca49b1ec00d58790ea7 "1.1A"), on an individual basis;
     3. (a) [deleted]
     4. (b) [deleted]
     5. (c) [deleted]
  2. (1A) every _CRR consolidation entity_ that:
     1. (a) has  _retail deposits_ equal to or greater than £75 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1B](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#8acf2f62eb084d28a585b3bc98f97be8); or
     2. (b) has _foreign assets_ equal to or greater than £10 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1A](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#d1cccea121134ca49b1ec00d58790ea7 "1.1A"),
  3. in each case, on the basis of its _consolidated situation_ ; and
  4. (2) a _ring-fenced body_ that is required to comply with Parts Two and Three of the _CRR_ on a _sub-consolidated basis_ and that:
     1. (a) has  _retail deposits_ equal to or greater than £75 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1B](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#8acf2f62eb084d28a585b3bc98f97be8); or
     2. (b) has _foreign assets_ equal to or greater than £10 billion, as determined in accordance with [1.1A](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#d1cccea121134ca49b1ec00d58790ea7 "1.1A"),
  5. in each case, on an _RFB sub-consolidated basis_.

*Effective: 01/01/2026*
  * [ Future  version of 1.1 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers/01-01-2027#ececa5b1d6e94241a462769859022c97)

1.1A

The _foreign assets_ thresholds referred to in [1.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#ececa5b1d6e94241a462769859022c97 "1.1") above are determined on the basis of the arithmetic mean of the value of _foreign assets_ held as at the three most recent _accounting reference dates_ of the _firm_ or _CRR consolidation entity_ (as applicable). If the _firm_ or _CRR consolidation entity_ has been in existence for less than three years, the period for the calculation is the period during which the _firm_ or _CRR consolidation entity_ has existed.

*Effective: 01/01/2023*

1.1B

The retail deposits thresholds referred to in [1.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#ececa5b1d6e94241a462769859022c97) above are determined on the basis of the arithmetic mean of the value of retail deposits held as at the three most recent accounting reference dates of the firm or CRR consolidation entity (as applicable). If the firm or CRR consolidation entity has been in existence for less than three years, the period for the calculation is the period during which the firm or CRR consolidation entity has existed.

*Effective: 01/01/2026*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_capital plan_

means the plan described in Chapter [6](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#dc8a6d0752934053a011b5e9a0686c3f "6").

_deposit_

has the meaning given in the Table of Part 2 of Annex II to Regulation (EU) No 1071/2013 of the European Central Bank of 24 September 2013 concerning the balance sheet of the monetary financial institutions sector as it had effect in EU law immediately before _IP completion day_.

_foreign assets_

means assets for which the counterparty is resident in a country or territory outside th _eUK_ that a _firm_ is required to report on row 0050 of [Annex X Template LV 44.00](/pra-rules/reporting-crr#990b7e370af54996bac0a996c9036291 "Annex X Template LV 44.00") of [Chapter 6](/pra-rules/reporting-crr#dd8f95a0599243e8b0fda195c8682971 "Chapter 6") of the [Reporting (CRR) Part](/pra-rules/reporting-crr "Reporting \(CRR\) Part").

_FPC_

means the Financial Policy Committee of the Bank of England.

_retail deposit_

means _deposits_ from “households” as defined in paragraph 42(f) of Part 1 of Annex V to the [Reporting (CRR) Part](/pra-rules/reporting-crr "Reporting \(CRR\) Part").

*Effective: 01/01/2023*

1.3

Unless otherwise defined, any italicised expression used in this Part and in the _CRR_ has the same meaning as in the _CRR_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Minimum Leverage Ratio

3.1

A _firm_ must hold sufficient _tier 1 capital (leverage)_ to maintain, at all times, a minimum _leverage ratio_ of 3.25%.

*Effective: 01/01/2022*

3.2

For the purposes of complying with [3.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#6b8df3bdcb1b474da0a0e6afabd19104 "3.1"), at least 75% of the _firm’s tier 1 capital (leverage)_ must consist of _common equity tier 1 capital_.

*Effective: 01/01/2022*

4

###  Countercyclical Leverage Ratio Buffer

4.1

A _firm_ must calculate a _countercyclical leverage ratio buffer_ of _common equity tier 1 capital_ equal to:

  1. (1) the _firm’s __institution-specific countercyclical capital buffer rate _multiplied by 35% with the product expressed as a percentage rounded to the nearest tenth of a percentage; multiplied by
  2. (2) the _firm’s_ _total exposure measure_.

*Effective: 14/12/2016*

4.2

A _firm_ must not count _common equity tier 1 capital_ that is maintained for the purposes of [3.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#6b8df3bdcb1b474da0a0e6afabd19104 "3.1") towards the calculation in [4.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#5735306f84704fe28caa525c7088ca5c "4.1").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4A

###  Additional Leverage Ratio Buffer

4A.1

If a firm has a G-SII buffer or an O-SII buffer, it must calculate an additional leverage ratio buffer of common equity tier 1 capital in accordance with the following formula:

\\[\mathrm{ ALRB=\left ( X \right )\left ( 0.35 \right )\left ( Y \right )}\\]

Where:

ALRB | = | the firm’s additional leverage ratio buffer;
---|---|---
X | = | the G-SII buffer rate or O-SII buffer rate that a firm is required to use to calculate its G-SII buffer or O-SII buffer or if the firm has both a G-SII buffer and an O-SII buffer, whichever buffer rate is higher; and
Y | = | the firm’s total exposure measure.

*Effective: 31/03/2025*

4A.2

A firm must not count common equity tier 1 capital that is maintained for the purposes of [3.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#6b8df3bdcb1b474da0a0e6afabd19104) or its countercyclical leverage ratio buffer towards the calculation in [4A.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#29675322224d4b0aa0490086f456be8a).

*Effective: 31/03/2025*

5

###  Notification

5.1

A firm must notify the PRA immediately if, at any time, it does not hold, or is likely not to hold, an amount and quality of capital that is:

  1. (1) necessary to comply with [3.1](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#6b8df3bdcb1b474da0a0e6afabd19104 "3.1"); and
  2. (2) equal to or greater than the sum of its countercyclical leverage ratio buffer and any additional leverage ratio buffer.

*Effective: 31/03/2025*

6

###  Capital Plan

6.1

When a firm is required to make a notification to the PRA under [5.1(2)](/pra-rules/leverage-ratio-capital-requirements-and-buffers#18e6d6ee50b24085bc7efbff77d2ba32 "5.1"), it must prepare a capital plan and submit it to the PRA no later than five business days after the firm identified that the notification was necessary.

*Effective: 31/03/2025*

6.2

The capital plan must include the following:

  1. (1) measures to secure that the amount of the firm’s common equity tier 1 capital is equal to or greater than the sum of the firm’s countercyclical leverage ratio buffer and any additional leverage ratio buffer; and
  2. (2) a plan and timeframe for the measures outlined for the purposes of rule 6.2(1).

*Effective: 31/03/2025*
