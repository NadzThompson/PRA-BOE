# Non–Solvency II Firms – Transitional Measures

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _run-off firm_ ;
  2. (2) a _non-directive insurer_ ; and
  3. (3) a _Swiss general insurer_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_application rules_

means any application rule in any Part in the [Non-Solvency II Firms](/pra-rules/non-sii-firms "Non-Solvency II Firms") Sector of the _PRA_ Rulebook.

_run-off firm_

means a _firm_ to which, but for the application of [Transitional Measures 2.1](/pra-rules/transitional-measures#ce01c09e148b43cda20c93d1e5e68a59 "Transitional Measures 2.1") to [2.3](/pra-rules/transitional-measures#69745ab3af33416894f8f44f0597f027 "2.3") in the [Solvency II Firms](/pra-rules/sii-firms "Solvency II Firms") Sector, the _Solvency II rules_ would apply.

_Solvency II rules_

means the _PRA_ rules in the [Valuation](/pra-rules/valuation "Valuation"), [Technical Provisions](/pra-rules/technical-provisions "Technical Provisions"), [Own Funds](/pra-rules/own-funds "Own Funds"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – General Provisions](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions "Solvency

Capital Requirement – General Provisions"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula "Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula"), [Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models "Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models"), [Minimum Capital Requirement](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement "Minimum Capital Requirement"), [Undertakings in Difficulty](/pra-rules/undertakings-in-difficulty "Undertakings in Difficulty"), [Investments](/pra-rules/investments "Investments"), [Composites](/pra-rules/composites "Composites"), [Conditions Governing Business](/pra-rules/conditions-governing-business "Conditions Governing Business"), [Insurance Special Purpose Vehicles](/pra-rules/insurance-special-purpose-vehicles "Insurance Special Purpose Vehicles"), [Group Supervision](/pra-rules/group-supervision "Group Supervision"), [Reporting](/pra-rules/reporting "Reporting"), [Surplus Funds](/pra-rules/surplus-funds "Surplus Funds"), [With-Profits](/pra-rules/with-profits "With-Profits"), [Insurance - Certification](/pra-rules/insurance---certification "Insurance - Certificatio"), [Insurance – Conduct Standards](/pra-rules/insurance---conduct-standards "Insurance – Conduct Standards"), [Insurance – Senior Management Functions](/pra-rules/insurance---senior-management-functions "Insurance – Senior Insurance Management Functions"), [Insurance – Allocation of Responsibilities](/pra-rules/insurance---allocation-of-responsibilities "Insurance – Allocation of Responsibilities") and [Insurance – Fitness and Propriety](/pra-rules/insurance---fitness-and-propriety "Insurance – Fitness and Propriety") Parts of the [Solvency II Firms](/pra-rules/sii-firms "Solvency II Firms") Sector of the _PRA_ Rulebook.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Transitional Applications of Rules in Relation to Run-Off Firms

2.1

Notwithstanding the _application rules_ , each Part in the [Non-Solvency II Firms](/pra-rules/non-sii-firms "Non-Solvency II Firms") Sector of the _PRA_ Rulebook applies to a _run-off firm_ for so long as it remains a _run-off firm_ as if the _firm_ was a _non-directive insurer_ and not a _UK Solvency II firm_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Reporting

3.1

A _firm_ must comply with the rules in:

  1. (1) [IPRU(FSOC)](/pra-rules/ipru-fsoc-interim-prudential-sourcebook-for-friendly-societies "IPRU\(FSOC\)") Chapter 5; and
  2. (2) [IPRU(INS)](/pra-rules/ipru-ins-interim-prudential-sourcebook-for-insurers "IPRU\(INS\)") Chapters 9 and 12

of the _PRA Handbook_ as at 31 December 2015, as they were applicable to the _firm_ (including any _waiver_) at that date, in respect of _financial years_ ending on or before 30 June 2016.

*Effective: 01/07/2016*
