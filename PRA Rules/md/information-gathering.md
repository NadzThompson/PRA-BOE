# Information Gathering

1

###  Application

1.1

This Part applies to every _firm_.

*Effective: 19/06/2014*

2

###  Cooperation

2.1

This Chapter does not apply to a _UK Solvency II firm_, the _Society_ or _managing agents_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.2

A _firm_ must take reasonable steps to ensure that its agents, _appointed representatives_ and suppliers under _material outsourcing_ arrangements deal in an open, cooperative and timely way with the _PRA_ in the discharge of its functions under any _relevant legislation_ in relation to the _firm_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Access to Premises

3.1

[3.3](/pra-rules/information-gathering#f1a9566d6a8a4197a604be616c201dfc "3.3") does not apply to a _UK Solvency II firm_ , the _Society_ or _managing agents_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.2

A _firm_ must permit any representative or appointee of the _PRA_ to have access, with or without notice, during reasonable business hours to any of its business premises, in relation to the discharge of the _PRA’s_ functions under any _relevant legislation_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.3

A _firm_ must take reasonable steps to ensure that its agents, _appointed representatives_ and suppliers under _material outsourcing_ arrangements permit any representative or appointee of the _PRA_ to have access, with or without notice, during reasonable business hours to any of its business premises, in relation to the discharge of the _PRA’s_ functions under any _relevant legislation_ in relation to the _firm_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4

###  Access to Documents and Personnel

4.1

Subject to section 413 of _FSMA_ , a _firm_ must, in relation to the discharge of the _PRA’s_ functions under any _relevant legislation_ :

  1. (1) permit any representative or appointee of the _PRA_ to have access to any document that the _PRA_ reasonably requests;
  2. (2) make itself readily available to meet any representative or appointee of the _PRA_ as the _PRA_ reasonably requests; and
  3. (3) answer truthfully, fully and promptly all questions reasonably put to it by any representative or appointee of the _PRA_.

*Effective: 19/06/2014*

4.2

Subject to section 413 of _FSMA_ , a _firm_ must, in relation to the discharge of the _PRA’s_ functions under any _relevant legislation_ , take reasonable steps to ensure that the following _persons_ act in the manner set out in [4.1](/pra-rules/information-gathering#5382ee0ab9fe44f4bf4e2dc5edf7dfc8 "4.1") in relation to the _firm_ :

  1. (1) its _employees_ , agents and _appointed representatives_ ; and
  2. (2) any other members of its _group_ , and their _employees_ and agents.

*Effective: 19/06/2014*

5

###  Information Requested on Behalf of Other Regulators

5.1

Subject to section 413 of _FSMA_ , a _firm_ must cooperate with the _PRA_ in providing information at the request of another regulator to enable that regulator to discharge its functions properly.

*Effective: 19/06/2014*
