# Insurance Company - Technical Provisions

1

###  Application

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _non-directive insurer_ , other than a _non-directive friendly society_ ; and
  2. (2) subject to [1.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5ef4b5db292d43cc88ce9f6c4eb1446d "1.2"), a _Swiss general insurer_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

This Part only applies to a _Swiss general insurer_ in respect of the activities of the _firm_ carried on from a _branch_ in the _UK_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.3

This Part applies to a _firm_ in relation to the whole of its business, except where a particular provision provides for a narrower scope.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.4

Where a _firm_ carries on both _long-term insurance business_ and _general insurance business_ , this Part applies separately to each type of business.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2

###  Establishing Technical Provisions

2.1

For _general insurance business_ , a _firm_ must establish adequate _technical provisions_ in accordance with[ Insurance Company – Overall Resources and Valuation 3.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company-overall-resources-and-valuation#24116ddbd8bf4bfc81439fdf161b7938 " Insurance Company – Overall Resources and Valuation 3.1").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.2

For _long-term insurance business_ , a _firm_ must establish adequate _technical provisions_ in respect of its _contracts of long-term insurance_ as follows:

  1. (1) for liabilities in respect of such contracts that have fallen due, in accordance with [Insurance Company – Overall Resources and Valuation 3.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company-overall-resources-and-valuation#24116ddbd8bf4bfc81439fdf161b7938 "Insurance Company – Overall Resources and Valuation 3.1");
  2. (2) otherwise, _mathematical reserves_ in accordance with the[ Insurance Company – Mathematical Reserves](/pra-rules/insurance-company-mathematical-reserves " Insurance Company – Mathematical Reserves Part") Part of the _PRA_ Rulebook and with due regard to generally accepted actuarial practice.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Risk Transfer Principle

3.1

A _firm_ may only take credit for _reinsurance_ (including all contracts of _reinsurance_ with an _ISPV_ and _analogous non-reinsurance financing agreements_) if and to the extent that there has been an effective transfer of risk from the _firm_ to a third party that is effective in all circumstances in which the _firm_ may wish to rely upon the transfer.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4

###  Assets of a Value Sufficient to Cover Technical Provisions and Other Liabilities

4.1

Subject to [4.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#d955a1fa983d4aadaa62b3948fc32884 "4.3"), a _firm_ (other than a _composite firm_) must hold _admissible assets_ of a value at least equal to the amount of:

     1. (1) the _technical provisions_ that it is required to establish under:
        1. (a) [2.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#35613f69f21f401e8b173873e61d1d66 "2.1"); or
        2. (b) [2.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#d0ae5cbc3bb346379985cdcbe18d3b9e "2.2"); and
     2. (2) its other:
        1. (a) _general insurance liabilities_ ; or
        2. (b) _long-term insurance liabilities_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.2

Subject to [4.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#d955a1fa983d4aadaa62b3948fc32884 "4.3"), a _composite firm_ must ensure that it holds;

  1. (1) _admissible assets_ separately identified in accordance with [Insurance Company – Internal Contagion Risk 4.1 ](/pra-rules/insurance-company---internal-contagion-risk#46b6e8435fe342e39cc1a2c718ca4ff2 "Insurance Company – Internal Contagion Risk 4.1 ")of a value at least equal to [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1")(1)(a) and [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1\(2\)\(a\)")(2)(a); and
  2. (2) other _admissible assets_ (other than those excluded under [4.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#d955a1fa983d4aadaa62b3948fc32884 "4.3")) of a value at least equal to [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1\(1\)\(b\)")(1)(b) and [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1\(2\)\(b\)")(2)(b).

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.3

For the purposes of [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1") and [4.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#3e79966d9d6442c386ef05c9ebe38d8c "4.2"), a _firm_ (other than a _pure reinsurer_) must exclude _property-linked liabilities_ and _index-linked liabilities_ and the assets held to cover them under [Insurance Company – Risk Management 4](/pra-rules/insurance-company-risk-management#feb4dfa07dbe4b369b287856431699d3 "Insurance Company – Risk Management 4").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.4

For the purpose of determining the value of assets available to meet _technical provisions_ and other _long-term insurance liabilities_ in accordance with [4.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#5f6cdfc4bebb446082d3d301b6b7203a "4.1"), [4.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#3e79966d9d6442c386ef05c9ebe38d8c "4.2") and [Non-Solvency II Firms - With-Profits 2.1](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms-with-profits#29586ca472d84202b85057e60d7e1933 "Non-Solvency II Firms - With-Profits 2.1"), to the extent already offset in the calculation of _technical provisions_ , no value is to be attributed to:

  1. (1) debts owed by _reinsurers_ ;
  2. (2) a claim under a _contract of insurance_ ;
  3. (3) tax recoveries; or
  4. (4) claims against _compensation funds_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5

###  Localisation

5.1

This Chapter does not apply:

  1. (1) to a _Swiss general insurer_ ;
  2. (2) in respect of debts owed by _reinsurers_ ;
  3. (3) in respect of _insurance business_ carried on by a _UK firm_ outside the _UK_; or
  4. (4) in respect of _general insurance business class_ groups 3 (Marine and transport) and 4 (Aviation) of [Insurance Company – Reporting 12.7](/pra-rules/insurance-company---reporting#c2fa932f98824d2a89ad0b31b0f43ebd "Insurance Company – Reporting 12.7").

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

5.2

In accordance with [5.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#a15211b42e4b42298ddb7053c0b1fe84 "5.3"), a _firm_ must hold _admissible assets_ held pursuant to [Insurance Company – Risk Management 3.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company-risk-management#37ed97d09f0d47efbfffd78c1b49f0f0 "nsurance Company – Risk Management 3.2"):

  1. (1) (where the _admissible assets_ cover _technical provisions_ in _UK_ sterling), in the _UK_ ; and
  2. (2) (where the _admissible assets_ cover _technical provisions_ in any currency other than _UK_ sterling), in the _UK_ or in the country of that currency.

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

5.3

For the purposes of [5.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#a279b32707a74771b16ed55125e9f09c "5.2"):

  1. (1) a tangible asset is to be treated as held in the country or territory where it is situated;
  2. (2) an _admissible asset_ consisting of a claim against a debtor is to be treated as held in any country or territory where it can be enforced by legal action;
  3. (3) a _security_ which is _listed_ is to be treated as held in any country or territory where there is a _regulated market_ on which the _security_ is dealt; and
  4. (4) a _security_ which is not _listed_ is to be treated as held in the country or territory in which the _issuer_ has its head office.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6

###  Matching of Assets and Liabilities

6.1

This Chapter does not apply:

  1. (1) to a _pure reinsurer_ ; or
  2. (2) in respect of assets held to cover _index-linked liabilities_ or _property-linked liabilities_ , except that, where the _linked long-term contract of insurance_ in question includes a guarantee of investment performance or some other guaranteed benefit, this Chapter will nevertheless apply to assets held to cover that guaranteed element.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.2

Assets held by a _firm_ to cover its _technical provisions_ and other _long-term insurance liabilities_ or _general insurance liabilities_ must:

  1. (1) have characteristics of safety, yield and marketability which are appropriate to the type of business carried on by the _firm_ ;
  2. (2) be diversified and adequately spread; and
  3. (3) in accordance with [6.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#9b28e1a2aa3c4115ab564e4614df8db2 "6.3"), be of a sufficient amount, and of an appropriate currency and term, to ensure that the cash inflows from those assets will meet the expected cash outflows from the _firm's_ insurance liabilities as they become due.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.3

For the purpose of [6.2(3)](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#be10cd3232c24b2fadfe2d60e5d64e8d "6.2\(3\)"), in determining expected cash outflows, a _firm_ must take into consideration any options which exist in the _firm's contracts of insurance_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

7

###  Premiums for New Business

7.1

A _firm_ must not enter into a _contract of long-term insurance_ unless it is satisfied on reasonable actuarial assumptions that:

  1. (1) the _premiums_ receivable and the investment income expected to be earned from those _premiums_ ; and
  2. (2) the _reinsurance_ arrangements made in respect of the risk or risks covered by that new contract;

are sufficient to enable it, when taken together with the _firm's_ other resources, to meet the requirements in [7.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#9906da13acf6464bb306e72b5196cddc "7.2").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

7.2

The requirements referred to in [7.1](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#1a8b857b23684054ba62f2411f39a522 "7.1") are, to:

  1. (1) establish adequate _technical provisions_ as required by [2.2](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#d0ae5cbc3bb346379985cdcbe18d3b9e "2.2");
  2. (2) hold _admissible assets_ of a value at least equal to the amount of the _technical provisions_ and other _long-term insurance liabilities_ as required by [4](/pra-rules/insurance-company---technical-provisions#241e61c55e1d417688acb419da680412 "4"); and
  3. (3) maintain adequate overall financial resources as required by [Insurance Company - Overall Resources and Valuation 2.3](/pra-rules/insurance-company-overall-resources-and-valuation#66aa5697f6c44f65bf796b2acc5a4fd3 "Insurance Company - Overall Resources and Valuation 2.3").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
