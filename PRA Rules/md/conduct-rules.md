# Conduct Rules

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

  1. (1) This Part applies to every function a __person__ (P) performs in relation to a _firm _(A) that is:
     1. (a) a _CRR firm_ ;
     2. (b) a _credit union_ ; or
     3. (c) a _third country CRR firm_ in relation to:
        1. (i) the activities of its establishment in the _UK_ ; or
        2. (ii) if it does not have an establishment in the _UK_ , activities in the _UK_.
  2. (2) This Part only applies if P:
     1. (a) is approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ to perform a _senior management function_ in relation to A;
     2. (b) is an _employee_ of A that should have been so approved;
     3. (c) is an _employee_ or _director_ who is performing a function that would have been a controlled function but for [Senior Management Functions 2.3](/pra-rules/senior-management-functions#341993027e134deca39941fc85cb3e7f "Senior Management Functions 2.3");
     4. (d) performs a _certification function_ in relation to A;
     5. (e) is a _Conduct Rules non-executive director_ of A; or
     6. (f) is a _person_ in relation to whom a notice under _section 59ZZA_ has been or could be given by the _PRA_ to an _authorised person_.
  3. (3) [3.1](/pra-rules/conduct-rules#e49509a0167e4d67861fedff886a7428 "3.1") to [3.3](/pra-rules/conduct-rules#5ad723607dbf430caa934461741e2a1c "3.3") only apply to a _person_ in (2)(a), (b), or (f).
  4. (4) [3.4](/pra-rules/conduct-rules#b6d1dd59bf614ff986342d49d317ca8e "3.4") only applies to a _person_ in (2)(a), (b), (e) or (f).

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_director_

means, in relation to a _firm_ , a _person_ who is a member of the board of directors of the _firm_ or, if there is no such board, the equivalent body responsible for the management of the _firm_.

_employee_

has the meaning given in [Certification ](/pra-rules/certification#2055307212bd44c7906411081040b335 "Certification 1.3")[1.2](/pra-rules/certification#4004e9bf25644a3c98160b704fee54dd "1.2").

_senior management function_

means either a _PRA senior management function_ or an _FCA designated senior management function_.

*Effective: 07/11/2017*

2

###  Individual Conduct Rules

2.1

**Individual Conduct Rule 1** : You must act with integrity.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.2

**Individual Conduct Rule 2:** You must act with due skill, care and diligence.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.3

**Individual Conduct Rule 3** : You must be open and co-operative with the _FCA_ , the _PRA_ and other regulators.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3

###  Senior Manager Conduct Rules

3.1

**Senior Manager Conduct Rule 1:** You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible is controlled effectively.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.2

**Senior Manager Conduct Rule 2** : You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible complies with the relevant requirements and standards of the _regulatory system_.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.3

**Senior Manager Conduct Rule 3** : You must take reasonable steps to ensure that any delegation of your responsibilities is to an appropriate _person_ and that you oversee the discharge of the delegated responsibility effectively.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.4

**Senior Manager Conduct Rule 4:** You must disclose appropriately any information of which the _FCA_ or _PRA_ would reasonably expect notice.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*
