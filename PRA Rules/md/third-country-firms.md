# Third Country Firms

1

###  Application and Definition

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies only to:

  1. (1) [deleted.]
  2. (2) a _third country firm._
  3. that is:
  4. (3) a _bank_ ; or
  5. (4) a _designated investment firm._

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.2

In this Part the following definition shall apply:

_resolution plan

_

means, in relation to a _firm_ , the plan for the resolution of the _firm_ prepared by the authority in the jurisdiction of the _firm’s_ head office responsible for the _firm’s_ resolution.

*Effective: 05/09/2014*

2

###  Adequacy of Resolution Plan

2.1

This chapter applies only to a _third country firm_ that is:

  1. (1) a _bank_ ; or
  2. (2) a _designated investment firm_.

*Effective: 05/09/2014*

2.2

A _firm_ must take all steps within its control to ensure that its _resolution plan_ provides adequately for the resolution of the _firm’s UK_ branch.

*Effective: 05/09/2014*
