# Reporting Pillar 2

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

This Part applies to:

  1. (1) every _firm_ that is a _CRR firm_ ; and
  2. (2) a _CRR consolidation entity_.

*Effective: 17/09/2021*

1.2

A _firm_ that is neither a _subsidiary_ of a _parent undertaking_ incorporated in or formed under the law of any part of the _UK_ nor a _parent undertaking_ must comply with this Part on an individual basis.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 1.2 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#b39534545d914060834d5846b35921f2)

1.3

A _firm_ that is not a member of a _consolidation group_ must comply with this Part on an individual basis.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 1.3 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#2dfe76a6c2c347d8be91586279d79ad6)

*Effective: 17/09/2021*

1.5A

A _CRR consolidation entity_ must comply with this Part on a _consolidated basis_ , and for that purpose, references to a _firm_ in this Part (other than in [1.1](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/03-03-2026#f39492af7a844254ac90c1c59f808366 "1.1") and 1.5A) mean a _CRR consolidation entity_.

*Effective: 17/09/2021*
  * [ Future  version of 1.5A after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#e2802ac8b9a745f686134492867df5da)

1.6

In this Part the following definitions shall apply:

_Advanced Measurement Approach_

means the advanced measurement approach referred to in Article 312(2) of the _CRR_.

_defined benefit pension scheme_

means an _occupational pension scheme_ with benefits defined independently of the _firm’s_ contributions as employer and investment returns.

_IRB Approach_

has the meaning given in article 143(1) of the _CRR_.

_occupational pension scheme_

has the meaning given in article 3(1) of the _Regulated Activities Order_.

_Standardised Approach to credit risk_

means the approach to credit risk set out in Chapter 2 of Title II of _CRR_.

*Effective: 01/01/2026*

2

###  Pillar 2 Reporting Requirements

2.1

A _firm_ must complete the _data item_ FSA071 for the risk assessments required in the [ICAA](/pra-rules/internal-capital-adequacy-assessment/03-03-2026 "ICAA") Part.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 2.1 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#b26b099786654fbaa5c3d01355db9f78)

2.2

A _firm_ must complete the _data items_ FSA078 and FSA079 for concentration risk.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 2.2 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#08ec8e8871fa447d8db897a470c9255d)

2.3

A significant _firm_ and any _firm_ that is not significant but that has permission from the _PRA_ to use the _Advanced Measurement Approach_ must complete the _data items_ FSA072, FSA073, FSA074 and FSA075 for operational risk, unless the data required in that _data item_ has already been reported to the _PRA_ by other means.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 2.3 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#82627372226440fbb65616e0fe7d7b6b)

2.4

A _firm_ with significant illiquid risk in its trading book must complete the _data item_ FSA080 for market risk, unless the data required in that _data item_ has already been reported to the _PRA_ by other means.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
  * [ Future  version of 2.4 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#55e3aa5e5b3b46a598d9bfe711fd09f7)

2.5

A _firm_ with permission from the _PRA_ to use the _IRB Approach_ for retail claims or contingent retail claims must complete the _data item_ FSA082 for credit risk that relates to the _IRB Approach_ for retail exposures.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.6

A _firm_ with a _defined benefit pension scheme_ must complete the _data item_ FSA081 for pension obligation risk, unless the data required in that _data item_ has already been reported to the _PRA_ by other means.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.7

A _firm_ must complete the _data item_ FSA076 for any wholesale portfolio of exposures for which capital requirements are calculated using the _Standardised Approach to credit risk_.

*Effective: 01/01/2018*
  * [ Future  version of 2.7 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#b68fac3a6bce4b2ea8b167b6ad22a79c)

2.8

A _firm_ must complete the _data item_ FSA077 for any retail portfolio of exposures for which capital requirements are calculated using the _Standardised Approach to credit risk_.

*Effective: 01/01/2018*
  * [ Future  version of 2.8 after 01/01/2027  ](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/01-01-2027#1a610f65ab0a44869ac4fcf904d72a92)

2.9

A _firm_ with _total assets_ equal to or greater than £5 billion at the relevant level of consolidation used as the basis of its ICAAP must complete the _data item_ PRA111.

*Effective: 01/10/2018*

3

###  Submission

3.1

A _firm_ must submit the _data items_ it is required to complete by this Part to the _PRA_ at the same time as the _firm_ submits its _ICAAP assessment_ to the _PRA_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.2

A _firm_ that does not submit an _ICAAP assessment_ to the _PRA_ on an annual basis must submit the _data items_ it is required to complete by this Part to the _PRA_ on a regular basis that is proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm’s_ activities.

*Effective: 01/10/2018*

3.3

_Data items_ must be submitted to the _PRA_ by electronic means.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.4

When submitting the required _data item_ , a _firm_ must use the template for the _data item_ set out in _[Chapter 4](/pra-rules/reporting-pillar-2/03-03-2026#193aed7efd104ad6bc9906cd79b49fef "Chapter 4")_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
