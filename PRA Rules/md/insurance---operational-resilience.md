# Insurance - Operational Resilience

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ;
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_ , as modified by [9](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#54ccd85195b445c787988c298e7d0819 "9"); and
  3. (3) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), _managing agents_ , as modified by [9](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#54ccd85195b445c787988c298e7d0819 "9").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_external group end user_

means a _person_ who receives services and who is outside of the _group_ of which the _firm_ is a member.

_impact tolerance_

means the maximum tolerable level of disruption to an _important business service_ or an _important group business service_ as measured by a length of time and other relevant metrics.

_[important business service](../#glossary-term-7f394daf60a94ed3805b4aa25fa7333c "important business service")_

means a service provided by a _firm_ , or by another person on behalf of the _firm_ , to another _person_ which, if disrupted, could pose a risk to:

     1. (1) where the _firm_ is a _[relevant Solvency II firm](/praerror/404.html?item=web%3a%7bD329E72E-6885-4F26-9D6A-E6CE7EFBD027%7d%40en "relevant Solvency II firm")_, the stability of the _UK_ financial system;
     2. (2) the _firm’s_ safety and soundness; or
     3. (3) an appropriate degree of protection for those who are or may become the _firm’s_ _policyholders_.

_[important group business service](../#glossary-term-28d87ba434fd4277a1bd49cb0a4e4158 "important group business service")_

means a service provided by a member of the _firm’s_ _group_ (other than the _firm_) to an _external group end user_ which, if disrupted, could pose a risk to:

     1. (1) where a _[relevant Solvency II firm](/praerror/404.html?item=web%3a%7bD329E72E-6885-4F26-9D6A-E6CE7EFBD027%7d%40en "relevant Solvency II firm")_ is a member of the _group_ , the stability of the _UK_ financial system;
     2. (2) the _firm’s_ safety and soundness; or
     3. (3) an appropriate degree of protection for those who are or may become the _firm’s policyholders_.

_[relevant Solvency II firm](/praerror/404.html?item=web%3a%7bD329E72E-6885-4F26-9D6A-E6CE7EFBD027%7d%40en "relevant Solvency II firm")_

means a _firm_ which fulfils any of the following conditions:

     1. (1) the _firm’s_ annual gross written premium income exceeds £15 billion; or
     2. (2) the total of the _firm’s_ _technical provisions_ , gross of the amounts recoverable from _reinsurance contracts_ and _UK ISPVs_ , as referred to in [Technical Provisions 2.1](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#08f5099494124929a2967abc9f70b61a "Technical Provisions 2.1") to [2.3](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#57f380dba7c5408884fa0bfece76bdbf "2.3") exceeds £75 billion,

determined on the basis of the average annual amount assessed across a rolling period of three years, calculated by reference to the _firm’s_ _accounting reference date_ ; and where the _firm_ has been in existence for less than three years, the assessment will be made on the basis of the annual average amount for the period during which the _firm_ has existed (calculated by reference to the _firm’s_ _accounting reference date_).

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2

###  Operational Resilience Requirements

2.1

A _firm_ must identify its _important business services_ and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#86c6bfa2da2349489663f9ef96e912f5 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, its _important group business services_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.2

A _firm_ must set an _impact tolerance_ for each of:

  1. (1) its _important business services_ ; and
  2. (2) where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#86c6bfa2da2349489663f9ef96e912f5 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, its _important group business services_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.3

The _impact tolerance_ set for each _important business service_ or _important group business service_ must specify the first point at which a disruption to the _important business service_ or _important group business service_ would pose a risk to:

  1. (1) where the _firm_ is a _relevant Solvency II firm_, the stability of the _UK_ financial system;
  2. (2) the _[firm’s](../#glossary-term-c8c2638afbfe4ec2a4d936a5d365917f "firm’s")_ safety and soundness; or
  3. (3) an appropriate degree of protection for those who are or may become the _firm’s_ _policyholders_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.4

The _impact tolerance_ set for each _important business service_ or _important group business services_ must specify the length of or point in time, in addition to any other relevant metrics, for which a disruption to that _important business service_ or _important group business service_ can be tolerated.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.5

A _firm_ must ensure it can remain within its _impact tolerance_ for each _important business service_ in the event of a severe but plausible disruption to its operations.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.6

A _firm_ must comply with [2.5](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#6c48cf250add440999af86031f8e4b08 "2.5") within a reasonable time of the rule coming into effect and in any event by no later than 31 March 2025.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

3

###  Strategies, Processes and Systems

3.1

A _firm_ must have in place sound, effective and comprehensive strategies, processes and systems that enable it adequately to:

  1. (1) identify its _important business services_ and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#86c6bfa2da2349489663f9ef96e912f5 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, its _important group business services_ ;
  2. (2) set an _impact tolerance_ for each _important business service_ and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#86c6bfa2da2349489663f9ef96e912f5 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, each _important group business service_ ; and
  3. (3) identify and address any risks to its ability to comply with the obligation in [2.5](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#6c48cf250add440999af86031f8e4b08 "2.5").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

3.2

The strategies, processes and systems required by [3.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#5697d6a82f6c4c41b957df5a04a731d0 "3.1") must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm’s_ activities.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

4

###  Mapping

4.1

As part of its obligation under [3.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#06ff589b9277454682c53dc6ee52b1d6 "3.1"), a _firm_ must identify and document the necessary people, processes, technology, facilities and information required to deliver each of its _important business services_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5

###  Scenario Testing

5.1

As part of its obligation under [3.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#06ff589b9277454682c53dc6ee52b1d6 "3.1"), a _firm_ must carry out regular scenario testing of its ability to remain within its _impact tolerance_ for each of its _important business services_ in the event of a severe but plausible disruption of its operations.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5.2

In carrying out the scenario testing required by [5.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#08dc815a7a7f4a14ad1971d6dd0eef95 "5.1"), a _firm_ must identify an appropriate range of adverse circumstances of varying nature, severity and duration relevant to its business and risk profile and consider the risks to delivery of the _firm’s important business services_ in those circumstances.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5.3

The scenario testing required by [5.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#08dc815a7a7f4a14ad1971d6dd0eef95 "5.1") must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm's_ activities.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6

###  Self-Assessment

6.1

A _firm_ must prepare and regularly update a written self-assessment of its compliance with this Part and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#5090df25a1b3453a972090e1ade01115 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, [Group Supervision 22](/pra-rules/group-supervision#82555e4a50974c81aebfe9869ad6c9be "Group Supervision 22").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6.2

The content and level of detail of a _firm’s_ written self-assessment must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm’s_ activities and, where applicable, to the activities of the _group_ of which the _firm_ is a member.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6.3

A _firm_ must maintain, and be able to provide to the _PRA_ on request, a current version of its written self-assessment, together with all versions produced during the preceding three years.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7

###  Governance

7.1

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves the _important business services_ and _important group business services _identified by the _firm_ in compliance with [2.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#beaa21cde7e045319f296edb71f01671 "2.1") and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#5090df25a1b3453a972090e1ade01115 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, [Group Supervision 22.3](/pra-rules/group-supervision#f332c904f14d4144bb1a4d82700071af "Group Supervision 22.3").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7.2

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves the _impact tolerances_ set by the _firm_ in compliance with [2.2](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#2d5f2fcd58c74940a4d350fb15efb640 "2.2") and, where [Group Supervision 22.2](/pra-rules/group-supervision#5090df25a1b3453a972090e1ade01115 "Group Supervision 22.2") applies, [Group Supervision 22.3](/pra-rules/group-supervision#f332c904f14d4144bb1a4d82700071af "Group Supervision 22.3").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7.3

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves and regularly reviews the self-assessment required by [6.1](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#9b41cc1534e54c35a6dc56bd2ab39b3f "6.1").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

8

###  Group Arrangements

8.1

Where a _firm_ is a member of a _group_ , the _firm_ must ensure it accounts for any additional risks arising elsewhere in the _group_ that may affect the _firm’s_ ability to comply with [2.5](/pra-rules/insurance---operational-resilience#6c48cf250add440999af86031f8e4b08 "2.5").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

9

###  Lloyd’s

9.1

This Part applies to the _Society_ and _managing agents_ separately.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*
