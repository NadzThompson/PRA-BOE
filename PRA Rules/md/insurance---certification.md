# Insurance - Certification

1

###  Application and Definition

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ;
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_ , as modified by [5](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#7692905caa5747148ecca3b29d0bfbe3 "5");
  3. (3) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), a _managing agent_ , as modified by [5](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#7692905caa5747148ecca3b29d0bfbe3 "5");
  4. (4) a _third country branch undertaking_ (other than a _Swiss general insurer_) in relation to the activities of the _third country branch_ that are subject to the _regulatory system_ ;
  5. (4A) a _third country insurance services provider_ in relation to the activities carried out in the _UK_ that are subject to the _regulatory system_ ; and
  6. (5) a _UK ISPV_ , in accordance with [6](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#738737821e6849678cc77dfd4f9d34b8 "6").

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.1A

This Part does not apply to a _SRO firm_.

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.2

This Part does not apply to a _small run-off firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.3

In this Part, the following definition shall apply:

_employee_

in relation to a _firm_ , includes any _person_ within the description set out in section 63E(9) of _FSMA_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.3A

For the purposes of this Part, _large firm_ includes a _third country insurance services provider_ which would be a _large firm_ if the amounts specified in (a) and (b) of the Glossary definition are only those amounts relating to the activities carried out in the _UK_ by the _third country insurance services provider_.

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

1.4

This Part does not apply to a function performed by:

  1. (1) a _PRA approved person_ ;
  2. (1A) a _person_ in relation to whom a notice under _section 59ZZA_ has been given to an _authorised person_ ;
  3. (2) a _person_ who performs an _FCA controlled function_ ; or
  4. (3) a _non-executive director_ in relation to their _non-executive director_ function.

*Effective: 31/12/2020*

2

###  Certification Functions

2.1

For the purposes of this Part, the following are _certification functions_ :

  1. (1) a function performed for a _firm_ by a _key function holder_ at the _firm_ ;
  2. (2) a function performed by a _material risk taker_ at a _large firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

A function is only a _certification function_ to the extent that the function relates to a _regulated activity_ carried on by a _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3

###  Performance of Certification Functions

3.1

A _firm_ must take reasonable care to ensure that none of its _employees_ performs a _certification function_ under an arrangement entered into by the _firm_ in relation to the carrying on by the _firm_ of a _regulated activity_ , unless the _employee_ has a valid _certificate_ issued by the _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2019*

3.2

A _person_ does not perform a _certification function_ for a _firm_ under [2.1](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#6177f07795eb408ab53e11da8f678ea3 "2.1") if the _person_ is performing any _controlled function_ for that _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.3

To the extent that:

  1. (1) a firm appoints a _person_ to perform a function which, but for this rule, would be a _certification function_ ;
  2. (2) the appointment is solely to provide cover for a _certification employee_ whose absence is reasonably unforeseen; and
  3. (3) the appointment is for less than four weeks;

such a _person_ does not perform a _certification function_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4

###  Material Risk Takers

4.1

This Chapter applies to a _firm_ that falls within [1.1](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#ec0b8b0fc25b4a0cbba41308981a2b4a "1.1") and is a _large firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.2

A _firm_ must identify, in respect of the _firm_ , its _employees_ who are _material risk takers_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.3

A _firm_ must keep its identification of _material risk takers_ pursuant to [4.2](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#fbf9e3acb19b41248244e2cc62f538c6 "4.2") up-to-date.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.4

A _firm_ must keep a record of its reasoning for the identification of _material risk takers_ pursuant to [4.2](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#fbf9e3acb19b41248244e2cc62f538c6 "4.2").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

5

###  Lloyd’s

5.1

This Part applies to the _Society_ and _managing agents_ separately.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

6

###  UK ISPVs

6.1

[2.1](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#6177f07795eb408ab53e11da8f678ea3 "2.1")(2) does not apply to a _UK ISPV_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

6.2

[4](/pra-rules/insurance---certification#f3a31f4eefd242789847cf1deb8e42fa "4") does not apply to a _UK ISPV_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*
