# Surplus Funds

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to a _UK Solvency II firm_ carrying on _with-profits insurance business_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_with-profits assets_

means the assets in a _with-profits fund_ except those meeting liabilities in respect of non-profit insurance.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

2

###  Surplus Funds and Technical Provisions

2.1

A _firm_ shall not treat _surplus funds_ as insurance and _reinsurance_ obligations when valuing payments to _policyholders_ and _beneficiaries_ in the calculation of _technical provisions_ in accordance with [Technical Provisions 2](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#a45a4cc38d904695b95ce2025de1ee45 "Technical Provisions 2").

**[Note: Art. 78(3) and Art. 91(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.2

In order to comply with [2.1](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#87926c96662148b98c378f06d0414fec "2.1"), a _firm_ must calculate the amount, if any, of its _surplus funds_ in accordance with [3](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#b4f7772ae32442c3a63e3bcaef3e83f2 "3").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Calculation of Surplus Funds

3.1

A _firm_ must calculate the amount (if any) of its _surplus funds_ in relation to each of its _with-profits funds_ as follows:

  1. (1) the value of _with-profits assets_ ;
  2. less
  3. (2) the value of _with-profits policy liabilities_ ;
  4. less
  5. (3) the amount of any tax or other costs which will arise on the recognition of future shareholder transfers properly attributable to that _with-profits fund_ in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_ , to the extent that such amounts are not included in (2);
  6. less
  7. (4) the value of any other liabilities (not being liabilities attributable to _with-profits policies_ or any other policies in the _with-profits fund_) properly attributable to that _with-profits fund_ in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_ ; and
  8. less
  9. (5) the value attributable to any future shareholder transfers (calculated by reference to the benefits referred to in [3.5](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8178f5f18e6c4f2fbba0baae94527aef "3.5")(3) and in a manner consistent with the method of calculation specified in [3.4](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#6707684e1e1048d085e7693e7879210b "3.4")) relating to _with-profits polices_ in that _with-profits fund_ which may properly be made out of that _with-profits fund_ in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.2

A _firm_ must value its _with-profits policy liabilities_ (other than _future policy-related liabilities_) in accordance with [3.3](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8dbe60e12cf84cd6a93194a7b118aec9 "3.3") unless:

  1. (1) valuation under [3.3](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8dbe60e12cf84cd6a93194a7b118aec9 "3.3") does not adequately reflect the value of some or all of such liabilities; or
  2. (2) the _firm_ can demonstrate that valuation under [3.3](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8dbe60e12cf84cd6a93194a7b118aec9 "3.3") is impracticable in respect of some or all of such liabilities;

in which case the _firm_ must value such liabilities in accordance with [3.4](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#6707684e1e1048d085e7693e7879210b "3.4").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.3

Unless the circumstances set out in [3.2](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#16d659b885014094ad70e338c74d7dbb "3.2") apply, a _firm_ must calculate the value of its _with-profits policy liabilities_ (other than _future policy-related liabilities_) as the aggregate of the retrospective value, in respect of each _with-profits policy_ , of the following (treating items that increase the future liability to _policyholders_ as positive values and treating items that reduce the future liability to _policyholders_ as negative values):

  1. (1)_premiums_ received;
  2. (2) any investment income on, and any increases (or decreases) in, asset values;
  3. (3) any amounts representing permanent enhancements which the _firm_ will take into account when determining the benefits ultimately payable;
  4. (4) any past miscellaneous surplus (or deficit) which has been allocated;
  5. (5) any expenses incurred (or deductions made in respect of expenses);
  6. (6) any past deductions for the cost of guarantees and smoothing, options and provision of life cover and any other benefits provided;
  7. (7) any partial benefits paid or due (other than benefits in respect of which deductions have been made under (6));
  8. (8) any tax paid or payable which is properly attributable to that _with-profits policy_ in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_ ;
  9. (9) any amounts received (or paid) under contracts of _reinsurance_ or arrangements which have a similar financial effect, which relate to that _with-profits policy_ (other than in respect of benefits for which deductions have been made under (6)); and
  10. (10) any past shareholder transfers which are properly attributable to that _with-profits policy_ in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_ , but deducting from that aggregate value any implicit allowance for the value of future shareholder transfers.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.4

Where the circumstances set out in [3.2](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#16d659b885014094ad70e338c74d7dbb "3.2") apply, a _firm_ must calculate the value of its _with-profits policy liabilities_ (other than _future policy-related liabilities_) on a prospective basis as the aggregate of the net present values of the following expected future cash-flows in respect of each _with-profits policy_ :

  1. (1) future _premiums_ ;
  2. (2) expenses expected to be incurred (or deductions expected to be made in respect of expenses);
  3. (3) planned deductions for the cost of guarantees and smoothing, options and provision of life cover and any other benefits;
  4. (4) any benefits payable of the type (or substantially similar to the type) specified in [3.5](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8178f5f18e6c4f2fbba0baae94527aef "3.5");
  5. (5) any amounts receivable (or payable) under contracts of _reinsurance_ or arrangements which have a similar financial effect which relate to that _with-profits policy_ but excluding amounts in respect of benefits:
     1. (a) which have been allowed for under (3); or
     2. (b) for which deductions have previously been made with the result that they do not fall under (3); and
  6. (6) tax payable which is properly attributable to that with-profits policy in accordance with any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.5

The benefits referred to in [3.4](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#6707684e1e1048d085e7693e7879210b "3.4")(4) are:

  1. (1) all guaranteed benefits, including amounts guaranteed to be payable on death and maturity (or on other events), guaranteed surrender values and paid-up values;
  2. (2) declared bonuses to which the _policyholder_ is contractually entitled; and
  3. (3) future discretionary additions to guaranteed benefits and discretionary payments, in addition to the guaranteed benefits, which are expected to be made when the benefits under the _with-profits policy_ becomes payable but only if and to the extent they are additions to benefits or payments which, if the _firm_ had been able to effect the calculation required by [3.3](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#8dbe60e12cf84cd6a93194a7b118aec9 "3.3"), are consistent with those for which allowance would have been made in accordance with that calculation.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.6

A _firm_ must not attribute any charges to a _with-profits policy_ for the purposes of this Part unless permitted to do so by any relevant provisions of the _FCA Handbook_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4

###  Consistency of Calculation

4.1

A _firm_ must ensure that the valuations included in the calculations required to be carried out in accordance with [3.1](/pra-rules/surplus-funds#21279e48ea4b4c0299203f48faef2113 "3.1") are consistent with the valuation methodologies adopted for the calculation of its _technical provisions_ in accordance with [Technical Provisions 2](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#a45a4cc38d904695b95ce2025de1ee45 "Technical Provisions 2").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
