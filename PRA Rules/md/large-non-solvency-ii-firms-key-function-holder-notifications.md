# Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Key Function Holder – Notifications

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _large non-directive insurer_ ; and
  2. (2) a _Swiss general insurer_.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_key function holder notification form_

means Form M.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Key Function Holder Notification

2.1

[Deleted.]

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

A _firm_ must provide the information required by [Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Fitness and Propriety 4.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-fitness-and-propriety#a2a577a8dfb7425c974d60107e7b8061 "Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Fitness and Propriety 4.1") for each _key function holder_ as soon as reasonably practicable after the appointment of the _key function holder_.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.3

The _PRA_ directs that, subject to [2.4](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#56f4f18cf996402c829243e440994d02 "2.4"), the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#ea2815329bb546148d7f9bf81c0d5b84 "2.2") must be provided to the _PRA_ in the _key function holder notification form_ in accordance with [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#423a765638e848ada3fa544ee961375b "3.3").

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

2.4

The _PRA_ directs that:

  1. (1) where the _key function holder_ is to be approved by the _PRA_ to perform a _PRA senior management function_ , the _firm_ must provide the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#ea2815329bb546148d7f9bf81c0d5b84 "2.2") to the _PRA_ in whichever Form is required for the _PRA senior management approval application_ , pursuant to [Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 2](/pra-rules/non-solvency-ii-firms-senior-managers-regime-applications-and-notifications#cc8caf1fcb5345c391b6a28c0902f2fb "Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Insurance Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 2"), which must be accompanied by a _statement of responsibilities form_ ; and
  2. (2) where the _key function holder_ is to be approved by the _FCA_ to perform an _FCA controlled function_ , the _firm_ is not required to submit a _key function holder notification form_ to the _PRA_ to the extent the _firm_ provides the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#ea2815329bb546148d7f9bf81c0d5b84 "2.2") to the _FCA_ as part of the application to the _FCA_ , with a _statement of responsibilities form_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3

###  Procedure for Making Applications and Notifications

3.1

The _PRA_ directs that, save as required by [3.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#754cd27be8014b7088784686823493b3 "3.2") and [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#423a765638e848ada3fa544ee961375b "3.3"), a _firm_ must make any applications, notifications or submissions required by this Part by submitting the form specified using the _ONA system_.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.2

If, under any direction or rule in this Part:

(1) a _firm_ is required to make an application, notification or submission online; and

(2) the information technology systems used by the _PRA_ fail and online submission is unavailable for 24 hours or more,

until such time as facilities for online submission are restored, a _firm_ must submit the specified form in the way set out in [Notifications 7](/pra-rules/notifications#8e186d20f3cf4d218117e0780f104f7c "Notifications 7").

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.3

The notifications required under [2.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-key-function-holder-notifications#d17b05b1b6f7486384cc628c2b2f41f8 "2.3") should be submitted in accordance with [Notifications 7](/pra-rules/notifications#8e186d20f3cf4d218117e0780f104f7c "Notifications 7").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4

###  Forms
