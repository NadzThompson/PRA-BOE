# Solvency Capital Requirement - General Provisions

1

###  Application

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ;
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_ , as modified by [6](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#82ba6417b5084adba9b8bea96da3bfe4 "6") to [8](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#7aacb5ad79c7459d9ae411dbd0cbeed2 "8"); and
  3. (3) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), _managing agents_ , as modified by [8](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#7aacb5ad79c7459d9ae411dbd0cbeed2 "8").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2

###  Requirement to Hold Eligible Own Funds Covering the SCR

2.1

A _firm_ must hold _eligible own funds_ covering its _SCR_.

**[Note: Art. 100 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  General Provisions for the Calculation of the SCR

3.1

A _firm_ must calculate its _SCR_ either in accordance with the _standard formula_ or using an _internal model_ for which __internal model permission has been granted.

**[Note: Art. 100 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.2

A _firm_ must calculate its _SCR_ on the presumption that it will pursue its business as a going concern.

**[Note: Art. 101(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.3

A _firm’s_ _SCR_ :

  1. (1) must be calibrated to ensure that all quantifiable risks to which the _firm_ is exposed are taken into account, including at least the non-life _underwriting risk_ , life _underwriting risk_ , health _underwriting risk_ , _market risk_ , _credit risk_ , and _operational risk_ ;
  2. (2) must cover existing business, as well as the new business expected to be written over the following 12 _months_ ; and
  3. (3) with respect to existing business, must cover only unexpected losses.

**[Note: Art. 101(3)–(4) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.4

A _firm’s_ _SCR_ must correspond to the value-at-risk of its _basic own funds_ subject to a confidence level of 99.5% over a one-year period.

**[Note: Art. 101(3) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.5

When calculating the _SCR_ , _firms_ must take account of the effect of _risk-mitigation techniques_ , provided that _credit risk_ and other risks arising from the use of _risk-mitigation techniques_ are properly reflected in the _SCR_.

**[Note: Art. 101(5) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.6

Notwithstanding [3.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#1794bdb16e9e4d8381a99ea6f1c2f1ba "3.2") to [3.5](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#c9205674caeb41deb4bc8d6a3cdecb31 "3.5"), a _firm’s_ _SCR_ shall not cover the risk of loss of _basic own funds_ resulting from changes to the _volatility adjustment_.

**[Note: Art. 77d(6) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4

###  Frequency of Calculation of the SCR

4.1

A _firm_ must calculate its _SCR_ and report the result of that calculation to the _PRA_ at least once a year.

**[Note: Art. 102(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.2

For the purposes of [2.1](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#e7043e5aea3c402ab26e8f7528a0d5e6 "2.1"), a _firm_ must hold _eligible own funds_ which cover its last reported _SCR_.

**[Note: Art. 102(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.3

A _firm_ must monitor the amount of its _eligible own funds_ and its _SCR_ on an ongoing basis.

**[Note: Art. 102(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.4

If a _firm’s_ risk profile deviates significantly from the assumptions underlying its last reported _SCR_ , the _firm_ must recalculate its _SCR_ without delay and report it to the _PRA_.

**[Note: Art. 102(1) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

4.5

Where there is evidence to suggest that the risk profile of a _firm_ has altered significantly since the date on which the _SCR_ was last reported by it, if so requested by the _PRA_ , the _firm_ must recalculate its _SCR_.

**[Note: Art. 102(2) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5

###  Capital Add-on

5.1

A _firm_ must make every effort to remedy the deficiencies that led to the imposition of a _capital add-on_ arising as a result of an _internal model significant risk profile deviation_ or a _significant system of governance deviation_.

**[Note: Art. 37(3) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.1A

A firm must be able to, upon request by the PRA, submit a progress report to the PRA setting out the measures taken, and the progress made, to remedy the deficiencies that led to the imposition of a capital add-on arising as a result of an internal model significant risk profile deviation or a significant system of governance deviation.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

5.2

Except as provided in [5.3](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#07c48e44863f480d90dbc9dc962c87e4 "5.3"), the _SCR_ prior to the imposition of the _capital add-on_ , together with the amount of the _capital add-on_ imposed by the _PRA_ , will constitute the _firm’s SCR_.

**[Note: Art. 37(5) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.3

For the purposes of calculating the _risk margin_ , the _SCR_ of a _firm_ must not include any _capital add-on_ imposed as a result of a _significant system of governance deviation_.

**[Note: Art. 37(5) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6

###  Requirement to Hold Eligible Own Funds: Lloyd’s

6.1

This Chapter applies to the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.2

The _Society_ must ensure that _eligible own funds_ are held at Lloyd’s covering its _SCR_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.3

_Eligible own funds_ covering the _central requirement_ must be _eligible own funds_ attributable to the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.4

Where the _standard formula_ is used by the _Society_ to calculate the _SCR_ for Lloyd’s, the _Society_ must carry out the following process before it will be taken to have demonstrated that [6.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#686723b76e684e23a9d59937cf91d022 "6.2") is met:

  1. (1) _own funds_ attributable to a _member_ are to be compared with the _member’s_ notional _SCR_ derived pursuant to [8.4](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#2f1e79743fe34de7b5437afa5ae3dd88 "8.4");
  2. (2) where the _own funds_ attributable to the _member_ are less than or equal to that _member’s_ notional _SCR_ , such _own funds_ (but no additional _own funds_ attributable to that _member_ , including any _own funds_ which are greater than the _member’s_ notional _SCR_) are to be taken into account for the purposes of establishing compliance with [6.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#686723b76e684e23a9d59937cf91d022 "6.2"); and
  3. (3) to the extent the _own funds_ attributable to the _member_ are less than that _member’s_ notional _SCR_ derived pursuant to [8.4](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#2f1e79743fe34de7b5437afa5ae3dd88 "8.4"), the _Society_ must hold _own funds_ in respect of the difference.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

6.5

Where the _Society_ uses an  _internal model _for which internal model permission has been granted,  __the _Society_ must also ensure that _eligible own funds_ are held at Lloyd’s covering, for at least 99.5% of the scenarios taken into account in the _internal model_ for the purposes of meeting [Solvency Capital Requirement - Internal Models 12.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#3227e9fd25a54798b652d3cea86a5312 "Solvency Capital Requirement - Internal Models 12.2"), any diminution in _own funds_ at Lloyd’s arising from the impact of those scenarios.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.6

Where an  _internal model _for which internal model permission has been granted is used by the _Society_ to calculate the _SCR_ , for the purpose of meeting the requirement set out in [6.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#686723b76e684e23a9d59937cf91d022 "6.2"), the _Society_ may take account of _own funds_ attributable to a _member_ only to the extent of the diminution, if any, to those _own funds_ resulting from the application of risk scenarios taken into account in the _internal model_.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

7

###  General Provisions for Calculation of the SCR: Lloyd’s

7.1

This Chapter applies to the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

7.2

In calculating the _SCR_ for Lloyd’s, the _Society_ must ensure that the _SCR_ is calibrated so as to include:

  1. (1) all quantifiable risks to which _members_ are exposed as a consequence of those _members_ carrying on _insurance business_ at Lloyd’s; and
  2. (2) all quantifiable risks to which the _Society_ is exposed, including risks to the _central assets_ and _central liabilities_ ;

in the manner required by [3](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#e6b2ac4ac324496ba0d2d20b96b3a6db "3") (and, where an _internal model_ is used, in accordance with the [Solvency Capital Requirement - Internal Models](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models "Solvency Capital Requirement - Internal Models") Part of the _PRA_ Rulebook and where the _standard formula_ is used, in accordance with the [Solvency Capital Requirement - Standard Formula](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models "Solvency Capital Requirement - Standard Formula") Part of the _PRA_ Rulebook).

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

7.3

The _Society_ must calculate a _central requirement_ for Lloyd’s which meets [7.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#45c38a39ddd44b72ae6be512a536f950 "7.2")(2).

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

7.4

The _central requirement_ must take account of the risk that the _central assets_ may be used to meet deficiencies (as to amount or quality) in _own funds_ attributable to _members_ , such _own funds_ supporting _members’_ _insurance business_ at Lloyd’s.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

8

###  Syndicate Notional SCR and Member Notional SCR: Lloyd’s

8.1

This Chapter applies to _managing agents_ and, where specified, the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

8.2

A _managing agent_ must calculate a notional _SCR_ for each _syndicate_ which it manages.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

8.3

The notional _SCR_ for each _syndicate_ referred to in [8.2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#18bf397c40f44bdda339bbf24cc4961c "8.2") must be calculated using a methodology which is consistent with the method used by the _Society_ to derive the _SCR_ for Lloyd’s for the purposes of enabling the _Society_ to comply with the relevant provisions of this Chapter.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

8.4

The _Society_ must calculate a notional _SCR_ for each _member_ using the method of calculation chosen to calculate the _SCR_ for Lloyd’s for the purpose of [3.1](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#1296c1c317de48569e344b6686785bd0 "3.1").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

8.5

Where a _managing agent_ manages risks which are included in the Lloyd’s _SCR_ calculation, the _managing agent_ must promptly assist and provide all relevant information to the _Society_ for the purposes of the _Society_ complying with relevant provisions of _SCR Rules_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
