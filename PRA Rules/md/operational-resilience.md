# Operational Resilience

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated:

  1. (1) other than [Chapter 8](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#715f1be723f246dbb3e1a6a38bfb658f "Chapter 8"), this Part applies to every _firm_ that is a _CRR firm_ ;
  2. (2) [Chapter 8](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#715f1be723f246dbb3e1a6a38bfb658f "Chapter 8") applies to every _CRR consolidation entity_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_external group end user_

means a _person_ who receives services and who is not a member of the _CRR consolidation entity's_ _consolidation group_.

_impact tolerance_

means the maximum tolerable level of disruption for an _important business service_ or an _important group business service_ as measured by a length of time and any other relevant metrics.

_[important business service](../#glossary-term-9e37f2de5924407684947cb48c49994a "important business service")_

means a service provided by a _firm_ , or by another _person_ on behalf of the _firm_ , to another _person_ which, if disrupted, could pose a risk to:

     1. (1) where the _firm_ is, or is controlled by, an _O-SII_ , the stability of the _UK_ financial system; or
     2. (2) the _firm’s_ safety and soundness.

_[important group business service](../#glossary-term-f3129e0223394c4aad148787b65f9642 "important group business service")_

means a service provided by a member of the _CRR consolidation entity's_ _consolidation group_ to an _external group end user_ which, if disrupted, could pose a risk to:

     1. (1) where any member of the _CRR consolidation entity's_ _consolidation group_ is an _O-SII_ , the stability of the _UK_ financial system; or
     2. (2) the safety and soundness of any _CRR firm_ within the _CRR consolidation entity's_ _consolidation group_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

###  Operational Resilience Requirements

2.1

A _firm_ must identify its _important business services._

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.2

A _firm_ must set an _impact tolerance_ for each of its _important business services._

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.3

The _impact tolerance_ set for each _important business service_ must specify the first point at which a disruption to the _important business service_ would pose a risk to:

  1. (1) where the _firm_ is, or is controlled by, an _O-SII_ , the stability of the _UK_ financial system; or
  2. (2) the _[firm’s](../#glossary-term-c8c2638afbfe4ec2a4d936a5d365917f "firm’s")_ safety and soundness.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.4

The _impact tolerance_ set for each _important business service_ must specify the length of or point in time, in addition to any other relevant metrics, for which a disruption to that _important business service_ can be tolerated.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.5

A _firm_ must ensure it can remain within its _impact tolerance_ for each _important business service_ in the event of a severe but plausible disruption to its operations.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.5A

Where a _firm_ is a member of a _group_, the _firm_ must ensure it accounts for any additional risks arising elsewhere within its _group_ that may affect the _firm’s_ ability to comply with [2.5](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#e675c098fd8d414a93522890a9f5e63a "2.5").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

2.6

A _firm_ must comply with [2.5](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#e675c098fd8d414a93522890a9f5e63a "2.5") within a reasonable time of the rule coming into effect and in any event by no later than 31 March 2025.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

3

###  Strategies, Processes and Systems

3.1

A _firm_ must have in place sound, effective and comprehensive strategies, processes and systems that enable it adequately to:

  1. (1) identify its _important business services_ ;
  2. (2) set an _impact tolerance_ for each _important business service_ ; and
  3. (3) identify and address any risks to its ability to comply with the obligation under [2.5](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#e675c098fd8d414a93522890a9f5e63a "2.5").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

3.2

The strategies, processes and systems required by [3.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#ac1e1f75e0ea42a4ba802b7f9cf6b7c2 "3.1") must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm’s_ activities.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

4

###  Mapping

4.1

As part of its obligation under [3.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#ac1e1f75e0ea42a4ba802b7f9cf6b7c2 "3.1"), a _firm_ must identify and document the necessary people, processes, technology, facilities and information required to deliver each of its _important business services_.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5

###  Scenario Testing

5.1

As part of its obligation under [3.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#ac1e1f75e0ea42a4ba802b7f9cf6b7c2 "3.1"), a _firm_ must carry out regular scenario testing of its ability to remain within its _impact tolerance_ for each of its _important business services_ in the event of a severe but plausible disruption of its operations.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5.2

In carrying out the scenario testing required by [5.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#6af1dbc1331c45fe9c9614faf6b06215 "5.1"), a _firm_ must identify an appropriate range of adverse circumstances of varying nature, severity and duration relevant to its business and risk profile and consider the risks to delivery of the _firm’s important business services_ in those circumstances.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

5.3

The scenario testing required by [5.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#6af1dbc1331c45fe9c9614faf6b06215 "5.1") must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm's_ activities.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6

###  Self-assessment

6.1

A _firm_ must prepare and regularly update a written self-assessment of its compliance with this Part.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6.2

The content and level of detail of a _firm’s_ written self-assessment must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the _firm’s_ activities, and where applicable to the activities of the _consolidation group_ of which the _firm_ is a member.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

6.3

A _firm_ must maintain, and be able to provide to the _PRA_ on request, a current version of its written self-assessment, together with all versions produced during the preceding three years.

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7

###  Governance

7.1

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves the _important business services_ identified by the _firm_ in compliance with [2.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#e73f46dfdb044408a10c7fe4d964ba76 "2.1").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7.2

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves the _impact tolerances_ set by the _firm_ in compliance with [2.2](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#415d861ee9774fd9961871b48b7502c7 "2.2").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*

7.3

A _firm_ must ensure that its _management body_ approves and regularly reviews the self-assessment required by [6.1](/pra-rules/operational-resilience#f51856f180dd4182a3259d89dbd0de6e "6.1").

*Effective: 31/03/2022*
