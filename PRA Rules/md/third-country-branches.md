# Third Country Branches

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to _third country branch undertakings_ , except _Swiss general insurers_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_branch scheme of operations_

means a scheme containing the information required in [5.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#7c5caf2ce94c4921a1120248d70754a9 "5.1").

_composite third country branch_

means a _third country branch_ (except a _third country pure reinsurance branch_) that carries on both _long-term insurance business_ and _general insurance business_ in the _UK_.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.3

A _third country branch undertaking_ (except a _third country branch undertaking_ that has a _third country pure reinsurance branch_) must hold on deposit as security in the _UK_ with a _CRD credit institution_ assets of an amount equal to at least:

  1. (1) £600,000 for a third country insurance branch whose insurance business is limited to general insurance business, except in the case where all or some of the general insurance business classes 10 to 15 are covered, in which case the amount must be at least £875,000;
  2. (2)  £875,000 for a third country insurance branch whose insurance business is limited to long-term insurance business; or
  3. (3)  the sum of the amounts set out in (1) and (2) for a third country insurance branch that is a composite third country branch.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

###  Contents of the Branch Scheme of Operations

5.1

The _branch scheme of operations_ must set out the following:

  1. (1) the nature of the risks or commitments which the _third country branch undertaking_ proposes to cover;
  2. (2) the guiding principles as to _reinsurance_ ;
  3. (3) [deleted]
  4. (4) [deleted]
  5. (5) [deleted]
  6. (6) estimates of the cost of setting up the administrative services and the organisation for securing business, the financial resources to meet those costs and, where the risks to be covered are classified under paragraph 18 of Part 1 of Schedule 1 to the _Regulated Activities Order_ , the resources available for the provision of assistance;
  7. (7) information on the structure of the system of governance; and
  8. (8) for the first three financial years:
     1. (a) a forecast balance sheet;
     2. (b) estimates of the financial resources intended to cover the provisions referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851);
     3. (c) for _general insurance business_ :
        1. (i) estimates of management expenses other than installation costs, in particular current general expenses and commissions;
        2. (ii) estimates of _premiums_ or contributions and claims under any _contract of insurance_ ; and
     4. (d) for _long-term insurance business_ :
     5. a plan setting out detailed estimates of income and expenditure in respect of direct business, _reinsurance_ acceptances and _reinsurance_ cessions.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

###  Provisions for Insurance and Reinsurance Obligations

6.1

A _third country branch undertaking_ must establish adequate provisions to cover the insurance and reinsurance obligations assumed by the third country branch undertaking in the UK, calculated in accordance with [6.1A](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#286c4533d29d47dc86f32433110a3eef) to [6.1E](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#32a0e3abcd3841c6b4c5b82113db687c).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1A

A third country branch undertaking must calculate the provisions referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851):

  1. (1) such that the calculation makes use of and is consistent with information provided by the financial markets and generally available data on underwriting risks (market consistency);
  2. (2) in a prudent, reliable and objective manner;
  3. (3) taking into account the principles set out in [Valuation 2](/pra-rules/valuation#b1dd1f10c578445ebef4a906b3c2aee9) and [3](/pra-rules/valuation#a31e4a04c1b44a2d9ac87a6f9d9b02a5); and
  4. (4) subject to [6.1B](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#53335c9946d24db29336c1c2da201c7a), in accordance with:
     1. (a) [6.1C](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a74e89e76e894abf98ddf8beffa3bc78) to [6.1F](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#b98ab5e8a46e458abdec5c39403cc030);
     2. (b) [Technical Provisions 9](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#6b0307cfe9144b0087991029412953a2) to [12](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#6e751e9042524d27b1807034fa098542);
     3. (c) [Technical Provisions 14](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#1cdf39f7dc33478984f483f7f67ce9dc);
     4. (d) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 2](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#7082e464368f45058cbcdbac1d30d9a4) to [5](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#87a4877de00844cd9ec70ad5f535368f);
     5. (e) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 10](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#572df991539d4d5384475e9cabb5116b) to [12](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#ff449640662c4660a26806d4b46dae18);
     6. (f) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 23](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#45d6c103f48747ebadbe7f839950037e) and [24](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#702a775145e540d79ba37f2cf11fc029); and
     7. (g) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 26](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#eef7b5636cd5400894b7780e5ef2fcfd) and [27](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#e9b17d53d1304ac18469d503a3a48edf)
     8. where a reference to ‘technical provisions’ is to be interpreted as the provisions referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1B

In [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 2.1](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#26886d3955e747f6a3b5dde8fdafb34c), the reference to ‘and the risk margin’ is to be disregarded.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1C

The insurance and reinsurance obligations referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851) must be calculated using the branch best estimate unless [6.1E](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#32a0e3abcd3841c6b4c5b82113db687c) applies, in which case they must be calculated in accordance with [6.1E](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#32a0e3abcd3841c6b4c5b82113db687c).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1D

The branch best estimate must be calculated in accordance with:

  1. (1) [Technical Provisions 3](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#0c360db07aa44aaabc7ffa9291003735);
  2. (2) [Technical Provisions 5](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#711fa81a98434481935364dd55ff356a) and [8](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#e32f4739dbb44aab959922b3798429e6);
  3. (3) the [Matching Adjustment Part](/pra-rules/matching-adjustment);
  4. (4) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 6](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#4ace6da85e9c4635b9c3392c3d12689d) to [9](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#1c3b9f537e0048ba8c4accabf677cfe9) (other than [7.2](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#c66e59eaff7940188a1fa32cf914d035));
  5. (5) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 13](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#12975b2206934687ad2da861d370a0c1) to [21](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#a25afd1a34f34839864c8c01b6b8e90c); and
  6. (6) [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 25](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#a1faa85e36754a57b6e252d296f898a9),

where a reference to ‘best estimate’ is to be interpreted as branch best estimate and a reference to ‘technical provisions’ is to be interpreted as the provisions referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1E

Where:

  1. (1) future cash-flows associated with insurance or reinsurance obligations can be replicated reliably;
  2. (2) that replication is provided using financial instruments; and
  3. (3) those financial instruments have a reliable market value which is observable,

then the value of those future cash-flows must be determined on the basis of the market value of those financial instruments, and calculated in accordance with [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 22](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#440309df0a004bf3bf34d2c744cfde8e), where references to ‘[Technical Provisions 2.5(2)(a)](/pra-rules/technical-provisions#e388d61285c94e1583bdc360c64baa80)’ is to be interpreted as a reference to ‘6.1E of the Third Country Branches Part’ and references to ‘technical provisions’ are to be interpreted as the provisions referred to in [6.1](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#a610a120975b4f59a4ec08c77bda7851).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

6.1F

  1. (1) A third country branch undertaking must ensure that the branch best estimate, and the assumptions underlying the calculation of the branch best estimate, are regularly compared against experience.
  2. (2) Where the comparison in (1) identifies that a systematic deviation exists between the branch best estimate calculations and experience, the third country branch undertaking must make appropriate adjustments to the actuarial methods being used and/or the assumptions being made to ensure that the branch best estimate is calculated in accordance with [6.1A](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#286c4533d29d47dc86f32433110a3eef) to [6.1D](/pra-rules/third-country-branches#199c0d7a2f0c4288840cd1ff919292d9).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

###  Restriction of Business

12.1

A _third country branch undertaking_ except a _pure reinsurer_ must not carry on any commercial business other than _insurance business_ and activities directly arising from that business.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

12.2

A _third country branch undertaking_ that is a _pure reinsurer_ must not carry on any business other than the business of _reinsurance_ and related operations.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
