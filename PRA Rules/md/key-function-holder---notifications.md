# Key Function Holder - Notifications

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ;
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_ , as modified by [5](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#84fb1e066aa64752a8d129c3d124cbd5 "5");
  3. (3) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), a _managing agent_ , as modified by [5](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#84fb1e066aa64752a8d129c3d124cbd5 "5");
  4. (4) a _third country branch undertaking_ (other than a _Swiss general insurer_); and
  5. (5) a _UK ISPV_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

_key function holder notification form_

means Form M.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Key Function Holder Notification

2.1

[Deleted.]

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

A _firm_ must provide the information required by [Insurance – Fitness and Propriety 4.1](/pra-rules/insurance---fitness-and-propriety#63445bf69a1e4ae9a2a8009469e4d2f5 "Insurance – Fitness and Propriety 4.1") for each _key function holder_ as soon as reasonably practicable after the appointment of the _key function holder_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.3

The _PRA_ directs that, subject to [2.4](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#6ae1f403e47148e5ab691be66f6c3e4a "2.4"), the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#ae5560f778244dd690fda57d3bc3398a "2.2") must be provided to the _PRA_ in the _key function holder notification form_ in accordance with [3.3](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#eb53a6be7f9943ed83e5d4f9848fb120 "3.3").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

2.4

The _PRA_ directs that:

  1. (1) where the _key function holder_ is to be approved by the _PRA_ to perform a _PRA senior management function_ , the _firm_ must provide the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#ae5560f778244dd690fda57d3bc3398a "2.2") to the _PRA_ in whichever Form is required for the _PRA senior management approval application_ , pursuant to [Insurance - Senior Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 2](/pra-rules/insurance---senior-managers-regime---applications-and-notifications#cf386c0194624de5828a57391073f726 "Senior Insurance Managers Regime - Applications and Notifications 2"), which must be accompanied by a _statement of responsibilities form_ ; and
  2. (2) where the _key function holder_ is to be approved by the _FCA_ to perform an _FCA controlled function_ , the _firm_ is not required to submit a _key function holder notification form_ to the _PRA_ to the extent the _firm_ provides the information referred to in [2.2](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#ae5560f778244dd690fda57d3bc3398a "2.2") to the _FCA_ as part of the application to the _FCA_ , with a _statement of responsibilities form_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3

###  Procedure for Making Applications and Notifications

3.1

The _PRA_ directs that, save as required by [3.2](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#f292bb8cd11949ba90e42d403835ea26 "3.2") and [3.3](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#eb53a6be7f9943ed83e5d4f9848fb120 "3.3"), a _firm_ must make any applications, notifications or submissions required by this Part by submitting the form specified using the _ONA system_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.2

If, under any direction or rule in this Part:

  1. (1) a _firm_ is required to make an application, notification or submission online; and
  2. (2) the information technology systems used by the _PRA_ fail and online submission is unavailable for 24 hours or more,

until such time as facilities for online submission are restored, a _firm_ must submit the specified form in the way set out in [Notifications 7](/pra-rules/notifications#8e186d20f3cf4d218117e0780f104f7c "Notifications 7").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3.3

The notifications required under [2.3](/pra-rules/key-function-holder---notifications#f3d7140772584b25991a988480470f2e "2.3") should be submitted in accordance with [Notifications 7](/pra-rules/notifications#8e186d20f3cf4d218117e0780f104f7c "Notifications 7").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*
