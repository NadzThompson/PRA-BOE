# Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Conduct Standards

1

###  Application and Definitions

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _large non-directive insurer_ ;
  2. (2) a _Swiss general insurer_ ; and
  3. (3) in relation to any of the foregoing _firms_ , any _person_ who is:
     1. (a) a _Conduct Rules non-executive director_ ;
     2. (b) an _employee_ of a _firm_ who is a _key function holder_ ;
     3. (c) a _certification employee_ (other than a _key function holder_);
     4. (d) approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
        1. (i) the _PRA_ ;
        2. (ii) the _FCA_ , in relation to a _relevant senior management function_ ;
     5. (e) an _employee_ who should have been approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
        1. (i) the _PRA_ ; or
        2. (ii) the _FCA_ , in relation to a _relevant senior management function_ ; or
     6. (f) an _employee_ who is performing a function that would have been a _controlled function_ but for [Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Management Functions 2.4](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-senior-management-functions#d955c4d8bed54dcf826a8e9a622090ae "Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Senior Management Functions 2.4").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.2

In this Part the following definition shall apply:

_employee_

has the meaning given in [Large Non-Solvency II Firms - Certification 1.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#02c2ba77ee094693bfb5df1c67b722a9 "Large Non-Solvency II Firms - Certification 1.2").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Scope of Conduct Standards

2.1

If you are a natural person who is:

  1. (1) an _employee_ of a _firm_ who is a _key function holder_ ; or
  2. (2) approved under section 59 of _FSMA_ by either:
     1. (a) the _PRA_ ; or
     2. (b) the _FCA_ , in relation to a _relevant senior management function_ ,

you must comply at all times with all of the _conduct standards_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.1A

If you are a _Conduct Rules non-executive director_ , you must comply at all times with the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#4673591992c547d5b7abbcbd8be2c846 "3.1") to [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#c7331917618a4f529bd1e299fab3cf80 "3.3"), [3.7](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#fc711afa932945f58783f57fc05ad607 "3.7") and [3.8](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#17be6755ba3e4108968f3be292787e88 "3.8").

*Effective: 03/07/2017*

2.1B

If you are an _employee_ of a type specified in [1.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#fe075502bfb34ff099a20363d3a011e0 "1.1")(3)(c), (e) or (f) you must comply at all times with the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#4673591992c547d5b7abbcbd8be2c846 "3.1") to [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#c7331917618a4f529bd1e299fab3cf80 "3.3").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

A _firm_ must require:

  1. (1) any _person_ performing a _key function_ to observe the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#4673591992c547d5b7abbcbd8be2c846 "3.1") to [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#c7331917618a4f529bd1e299fab3cf80 "3.3");
  2. (2) any _key function holder_ (other than a _notified non-executive director_) to observe the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.4](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#d063413463d64c54a5a86ee55ddb5af4 "3.4") to [3.8](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#17be6755ba3e4108968f3be292787e88 "3.8"), in addition to complying with (1);
  3. (3) any _notified non-executive director_ to observe the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.7](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#fc711afa932945f58783f57fc05ad607 "3.7") and [3.8](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#17be6755ba3e4108968f3be292787e88 "3.8"), in addition to complying with (1); and
  4. (4) an _employee_ of a type specified in [1.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#fe075502bfb34ff099a20363d3a011e0 "1.1")(3)(c), (e) or (f) to observe the _conduct standards_ specified in [3.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#4673591992c547d5b7abbcbd8be2c846 "3.1") to [3.3](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-conduct-standards#c7331917618a4f529bd1e299fab3cf80 "3.3").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3

###  Conduct Standards

3.1

Individual Conduct Standard 1: You must act with integrity.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.2

Individual Conduct Standard 2: You must act with due skill, care and diligence.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.3

Individual Conduct Standard 3: You must be open and co-operative with the _FCA_ , the _PRA_ and other regulators.

*Effective: 07/03/2016*

3.4

Senior Manager Conduct Standard 1: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible is controlled effectively.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.5

Senior Manager Conduct Standard 2: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the _firm_ for which you are responsible complies with the relevant requirements and standards of the _regulatory system_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.6

Senior Manager Conduct Standard 3: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that any delegation of your responsibilities is to an appropriate _person_ and that you oversee the discharge of the delegated responsibility effectively.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.7

Senior Manager Conduct Standard 4: You must disclose appropriately any information of which the _FCA_ or the _PRA_ would reasonably expect to have notice.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.8

Senior Manager Conduct Standard 5: When exercising your responsibilities, you must pay due regard to the interests of current and potential future _policyholders_ in ensuring the provision by the _firm_ of an appropriate degree of protection for their insured benefits.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*
