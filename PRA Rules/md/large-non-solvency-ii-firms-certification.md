# Large Non-Solvency II Firms – Certification

1

###  Application and Definition

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _large non-directive insurer_ ; and
  2. (2) a _Swiss general insurer_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.2

In this Part, the following definition shall apply:

_employee_

in relation to a _firm_ , includes any _person_ within the description set out in section 63E(9) of _FSMA_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

1.3

This Part does not apply to a function performed by:

  1. (1) a _PRA approved person_ ;
  2. (2) a _person_ who performs an FCA controlled function; or
  3. (3) a _non-executive director_ in relation to their _non-executive director_ function.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2

###  Certification Functions

2.1

For the purposes of this Part, the following are _certification functions_ :

  1. (1) a function performed for a _firm_ by a _key function holder_ at the _firm_ ;
  2. (2) a function performed by a _material risk taker_ at a _large firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

2.2

A function is only a _certification function _to the extent that the function relates to a _regulated activity_ carried on by a _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3

###  Performance of Certification Functions

3.1

A _firm_ must take reasonable care to ensure that none of its _employees_ performs a _certification function_ under an arrangement entered into by the _firm_ in relation to the carrying on by the _firm_ of a _regulated activity_ , unless the _employee_ has a valid _certificate_ issued by the _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2019*

3.2

A _person_ does not perform a _certification function_ for a _firm_ under [2.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#8c5ea317b07b4b3b95e0595ea2264529 "2.1") if the _person_ is performing any _controlled function_ for that _firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

3.3

To the extent that:

  1. (1) a _firm_ appoints a _person_ to perform a function which, but for this rule, would be a _certification function_ ;
  2. (2) the appointment solely is to provide cover for a _certification employee_ whose absence is reasonably unforeseen; and
  3. (3) the appointment is for less than four weeks;

such a _person_ does not perform a _certification function_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4

###  Material Risk Takers

4.1

This Chapter applies to a _firm_ that falls within [1.1](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#e1a668d4b7a14ef0ba457bc90901e1ba "1.1") and is a _large firm_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.2

A _firm_ must identify, in respect of the _firm_ , its _employees_ who are _material risk takers_.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.3

A _firm_ must keep its identification of _material risk takers_ pursuant to [4.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#920938c0a52241f99482387382104ab4 "4.2") up-to-date.

*Effective: 10/12/2018*

4.4

A _firm_ must keep a record of its reasoning for the identification of _material risk takers_ pursuant to [4.2](/pra-rules/large-non-solvency-ii-firms-certification#920938c0a52241f99482387382104ab4 "4.2").

*Effective: 10/12/2018*
