# Minimum Capital Requirement

1

###  Application

1.1

Unless otherwise stated, this Part applies to:

  1. (1) a _UK Solvency II firm_ ; and
  2. (2) in accordance with [Insurance General Application 3](/pra-rules/insurance-general-application#2e5a9cd32e6d4026895a5d543b990108 "Insurance General Application 3"), the _Society_ , as modified by [5](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#457de76e62b14aeb9f3b33c94a31861b "5").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply: [Note: there are currently no Part specific definitions]

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

2

###  General Provisions

2.1

A _firm_ must hold _eligible own funds_ covering the _MCR_.

**[Note: Art. 128 of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3

###  Calculation of the Minimum Capital Requirement

3.1

[Deleted]

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.1A

The MCR must be equal to the following:

\\[\mathrm{MCR=max(MCR_{combined},\ AMCR)}\\]

where:

  1. (1) MCRcombined denotes the combined minimum capital requirement referred to in [3.1B](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#17ca7ea0a5944e638354fc1b6b6ae81f); and
  2. (2) AMCR denotes the absolute floor referred to in [3.2](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#965c1f3c028f41a3a7128379f39abac9).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.1B

The combined minimum capital requirement must be equal to the following:

\\[\mathrm{MCR_{combined}\ =\ min(max(MCR_{linear},\ 0.25\ \cdot \ SCR),\ 0.45\ \cdot \ SCR)}\\]

where:

  1. (1) MCRlinear denotes the linear minimum capital requirement, calculated in accordance with [3A](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#4098b14d755742deb1457676b47e4f14) to [3C](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#38f0a95f0ca74a108e6394f7685d25e9); and
  2. (2) SCR denotes the SCR calculated in accordance with SCR Rules.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.2

The _MCR_ must have an absolute floor of:

  1. (1) £2,400,000 for _firms_ , including _captive insurers_ , which have _Part 4A permission_ to _effect contracts of insurance_ or carry out contracts of insurance that are _contracts of general insurance_ , except in the case where all or some of the general insurance business classes 10 to 15 are covered, in which case it must be no less than £3,500,000;
  2. (2) £3,500,000 for _firms_ , including _captive insurers_ , which have _Part 4A permission_ to _effect contracts of insurance_ or _carry out contracts of insurance_ that are _contracts of long term insurance_ ;
  3. (3) £3,500,000 for _pure reinsurers_, except in the case of _captive reinsurers_ that are _pure reinsurers_ , in which case the _MCR_ must be no less than £1,200,000;
  4. (4) Unless (5) applies, the sum of the amounts set out in (1) and (2) for composite firms other than _pure reinsurers_ ; or
  5. (5) For composite firms, other than pure reinsurers, whose Part 4A permission in relation to general insurance business is limited to general insurance business class 1 (accident) or class 2 (sickness) and where the gross written premiums for either:
     1. (a)  general insurance business; or
     2. (b)  long-term insurance business,
     3. do not exceed 10% of total gross written premiums of the firm as a whole, the amount set out in (2).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3.3

Without prejudice to the requirements on the absolute floor in [3.2](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#965c1f3c028f41a3a7128379f39abac9 "3.2"), the _MCR_ must neither fall below 25% nor exceed 45% of the _firm’s SCR_, calculated in accordance with _SCR Rules_ , and including any _capital add-on_ which has been imposed.

**[Note: Art. 129(3) of the _Solvency II Directive]_**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

3A

###  Calculation of the Linear Minimum Capital Requirement

3A.1

The linear minimum capital requirement referred to in [3.1B](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#17ca7ea0a5944e638354fc1b6b6ae81f) must be equal to the following:

\\[\mathrm{MCR_{linear}\ =\ MCR_{(linear,gi)}\ +\ MCR_{(linear,l)}}\\]

where:

  1. (1) MCR(linear,gi) denotes the linear formula component for general insurance and reinsurance obligations; and
  2. (2) MCR(linear,l) denotes the linear formula component for long-term insurance and reinsurance obligations.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B

###  Linear Formula Component for General Insurance and Reinsurance Obligations

3B.1

The linear formula component for general insurance and reinsurance obligations referred to in [3A.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#1150acfd559a45118c26d652ec6141a1) must be equal to the following:

\\[\mathrm{MCR_{linear,gi}}=\sum_{s}\mathrm{(\alpha_{s}\cdot TP_{(gi,s)})+(\beta_{s}\cdot P_{s})}\\]

where:

  1. (1) the sum covers all segments set out in 6.1;
  2. (2) TP(gi,s) denotes the technical provisions without a risk margin for general insurance and reinsurance obligations in the segment s after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero;
  3. (3) Ps denotes the premiums written for general insurance and reinsurance obligations in the segment s during the last 12 months, after deduction of premiums for reinsurance contracts, with a floor equal to zero; and
  4. (4) the factors αs and βs are set out in [6.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#6c9cd61b035c450aa36f95067a2c6bca).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.2

The amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles referred to in [3B.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#855bb950ae824d12b302891793f59579)(2) must not include any of the following amounts:

  1. (1) amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts or special purpose vehicles that cannot be taken into account in accordance with [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 23.3](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#00354399677c45e28707c7ebb454e88e) and [23.5](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#96b729b1951c413aaa57f4c5f87ff829); and
  2. (2) amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts or special purpose vehicles, that do not comply with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula 3G2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#74b69e05078a475a8890378bb47d7b20), [3G3](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#c4e811b89df546fabb32b160719a7e8a), [3G5](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#822382cadd2e4d519a1bdbfaf9b1dc4d) and [3G7](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#29c171aff3dd43c2881367a85d365ab7) or with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models 11.10](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#00db638455f7400ca9fa2498ea219830) to [11.12](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#34ea2166e0ee4db2ad8acecd3701954a).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3B.3

In the calculation of premiums written after deduction of premiums for reinsurance contracts referred to in 3B.1(3), the following premiums for reinsurance contracts must not be deducted:

  1. (1) premiums in relation to non-insurance events or settled insurance claims that are not accounted for in the cashflows referred to in [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 23.3](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#00354399677c45e28707c7ebb454e88e); and
  2. (2) premiums for reinsurance contracts that do not comply with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula 3G2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#74b69e05078a475a8890378bb47d7b20), [3G3](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#c4e811b89df546fabb32b160719a7e8a), [3G5](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#822382cadd2e4d519a1bdbfaf9b1dc4d) and [3G7](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#29c171aff3dd43c2881367a85d365ab7), or with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models 11.10](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#00db638455f7400ca9fa2498ea219830) to [11.12](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#34ea2166e0ee4db2ad8acecd3701954a).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3C

###  Linear Formula Component for Long-term Insurance and Reinsurance Obligations

3C.1

The linear formula component for long-term insurance and reinsurance obligations referred to in [3A.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#1150acfd559a45118c26d652ec6141a1) must be equal to the following:

\\[\mathrm{MCR_{linear,l}\ =\ 0.037\ \cdot \ TP_{(l,1)}\ – 0.052 \cdot TP_{(l,2)} + 0.007 \cdot TP_{(l,3)} + 0.021 \cdot TP_{(l,4)} + 0.0007 \cdot CAR}\\]

where:

  1. (1) TP(l,1) denotes the technical provisions without a risk margin in relation to guaranteed benefits provided for long-term insurance obligations with profit participation, after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero, and technical provisions without a risk margin for reinsurance obligations where the underlying long-term insurance obligations include profit participation, after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero;
  2. (2) TP(l,2) denotes the technical provisions without a risk margin in relation to future discretionary benefits for long-term insurance obligations with profit participation, after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero;
  3. (3) TP(l,3) denotes the technical provisions without a risk margin for linked long-term liabilities, after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero;
  4. (4) TP(l,4) denotes the technical provisions without a risk margin for long-term insurance and reinsurance obligations other than those mentioned in (1) to (3), after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles, with a floor equal to zero; and
  5. (5) CAR denotes the total capital at risk, being the sum, in relation to each contract of insurance that gives rise to long-term insurance and reinsurance obligations, of the capital at risk of the contracts, where the capital at risk of a contract means the higher of zero and the difference between the following two amounts:
  6. (a) the sum of all of the following:
     1. (i) the amount that the firm would currently pay in the event of the death or disability of the persons insured under the contract of insurance after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles; and
     2. (ii) the expected present value of amounts not covered in (i) that the firm would pay in the future in the event of the immediate death or disability of the persons insured under the contract of insurance after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles; and
  7. (b) the best estimate of the corresponding obligations after deduction of the amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles.

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

3C.2

The amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts and special purpose vehicles referred to in [3C.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#decb85e240ac4140a40b1748c31791ed)(1) to (4), must not include any of the following:

  1. (1) amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts or special purpose vehicles that cannot be taken into account in accordance with [Technical Provisions – Further Requirements 23.3](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#00354399677c45e28707c7ebb454e88e) and [23.5](/pra-rules/technical-provisions-further-requirements#96b729b1951c413aaa57f4c5f87ff829); and
  2. (2) amounts recoverable from reinsurance contracts or special purpose vehicles that do not comply with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Standard Formula 3G2](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#74b69e05078a475a8890378bb47d7b20), [3G3](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#c4e811b89df546fabb32b160719a7e8a), [3G5](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#822382cadd2e4d519a1bdbfaf9b1dc4d), [3G6](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#7cbea4aabf164ac4a46c949e77b0a4cf), [3G7](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#29c171aff3dd43c2881367a85d365ab7), [3G8](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#e31932a279df4af1bb9b3107e21f87d4) and [3G9](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---standard-formula#03e875c8581c4871ba1360647f2c01b2) or with [Solvency Capital Requirement – Internal Models 11.10](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#00db638455f7400ca9fa2498ea219830) to [11.12](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---internal-models#34ea2166e0ee4db2ad8acecd3701954a).

*Effective: 31/12/2024*

4

###  Frequency and Reporting in Relation to the Minimum Capital Requirement

4.1

A _firm_ must calculate the _MCR_ at least quarterly and report the results of that calculation to the _PRA_ in accordance with the requirements laid down in [Reporting 2.1](/pra-rules/reporting#35c185cc25f44e2c8ebd1b6357d91e60 "Reporting 2.1") to [2.5](/pra-rules/reporting#1f0539ab8c374d4d9bedf3af19db6f28 "2.5").

**[Note: Art. 129(4) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 31/12/2021*

4.2

Where either of the limits referred to in [3.3](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#f85fa767a5c1471ca9f840ca14ec5adb "3.3") determines a _firm’s_ _MCR_ the _firm_ must provide the _PRA_ information allowing a proper understanding of the reasons therefor.

**[Note: Art. 129(4) of the _Solvency II Directive_]**

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5

###  Lloyd’s

5.1

This Chapter applies to the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.2

In calculating the _MCR_ for Lloyd’s, in the manner required by [3](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#dbc7c2ea8205487aab57b9ee703c87f7 "3"), the _Society_ must ensure that the _MCR_ is calibrated so as to include all quantifiable risks to which:

  1. (1) _members_ are exposed as a consequence of those _members_ carrying on _insurance business_ at Lloyd’s; and
  2. (2) the _Society_ is exposed, including risks to the _central assets_ and _central liabilities_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.3

The _Society_ must determine, at least quarterly, the ratio of the Lloyd’s _MCR_ to the Lloyd’s _SCR_ and notify the _PRA_ of the result at the same time it reports the quarterly _MCR_ calculation required by [4.1](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#939d7f0e452c4f4f9ab7b18e115e9220 "4.1").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.4

The _Society_ must calculate a reporting point for each _underwriting member_ , in accordance with [5.5](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#a9dcd70b666a439f85e20a2bb8b62c6c "5.5").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.5

The reporting point for each _underwriting member_ must be calculated using the ratio referred to in [5.3](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#7330794eaf504ad1bba1fa0e7f554669 "5.3"), expressed as a percentage of the _member’s_ notional _SCR_ referred to in [Solvency Capital Requirement – General Provisions 8.4](/pra-rules/solvency-capital-requirement---general-provisions#2f1e79743fe34de7b5437afa5ae3dd88 "Solvency Capital Requirement – General Provisions 8.4").

*Effective: 01/01/2016*

5.6

The _Society_ must notify the _PRA_ if _own funds_ attributable to a _member_ fall below the reporting point determined in accordance with [5.5](/pra-rules/minimum-capital-requirement#a9dcd70b666a439f85e20a2bb8b62c6c "5.5") as soon as it is observed by the _Society_.

*Effective: 01/01/2016*
